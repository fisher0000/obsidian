# 1. Executive summary

1. 行动纲要

The [ICH Q3A(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3A_\(R2\)_Impurities_in_new_drug_substances "EMA ICH Q3A (R2) Impurities in new drug substances") and [Q3B(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3B_\(R2\)_Impurities_in_new_drug_products "EMA ICH Q3B (R2) Impurities in new drug products") guidelines provide a framework for qualifying Non-Mutagenic Impurities (NMI) in drug substances (DS) and drug products (DP) for registration applications but offer limited guidance on newly identified or elevated impurity levels above existing ICH Q3A or Q3B impurity qualification thresholds (QT), that are identified in post-approval procedures after non-clinical toxicology studies have been completed for initial Marketing Authorisation Applications (MAAs). This reflection paper recognises the need for an adequate safety evaluation and, in line with the 3Rs principles, particularly the replacement of tests on animals, suggests alternative strategies to in vivo animal studies for qualifying novel or elevated levels of [impurities](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8 "分类:杂质") specified above ICH Q3A/Q3B QTs. ICH Q3A/Q3B states that impurities can be qualified when present in the DS/DP used in the non-clinical [repeated dose toxicity](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E9%87%8D%E5%A4%8D%E7%BB%99%E8%8D%AF%E6%AF%92%E6%80%A7 "分类:重复给药毒性") studies at a level that does not impact the outcome of the toxicological assessment of the DS/DP. Furthermore, impurities may be qualified when these are also present as metabolites in animals or humans. This reflection paper provides further recommendations on how to assess metabolism data for qualifying an impurity as well as recommendations on a similarity assessment between the impurity and the Active Pharmaceutical Ingredient ([API](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E5%8E%9F%E6%96%99%E8%8D%AF "分类:原料药")) to justify read across to existing toxicological studies with the DS.

ICH Q3A(R2)和Q3B(R2)指南为在注册申请中界定原料药（DS）和制剂（DP）中的非致突变性杂质（NMI）提供了框架，但对在初始上市许可申请（MAAs）的非临床毒理学研究完成后、在上市后程序中发现的新识别杂质或高于现有ICH Q3A或Q3B杂质界定阈值（QT）的杂质水平，提供的指导有限。本思考性文件认识到有必要进行充分的安全性评估，并依据3R原则，特别是替代动物实验的原则，建议采用替代体内动物研究的策略，用于界定上述ICH Q3A/Q3B界定阈值（QTs）以上的新杂质或升高水平的杂质。ICH Q3A/Q3B指出，当杂质存在于非临床重复给药毒性研究中所使用的原料药/制剂中，且其水平不会影响对原料药/制剂的毒理学评估结果时，可对杂质进行界定。此外，当这些杂质也作为代谢产物存在于动物或人体中时，可能可以进行界定​​​⁠⁠⁠⁠​⁠​​​⁠。本思考性文件进一步提供了如何评估代谢数据以界定杂质以及杂质与原料药（DS）之间相似性评估的建议，以支持将现有毒理学研究结果外推至该杂质。

The need for additional data depends on the level of concern. The level of concern for an NMI is affected in a multifactorial manner, including intake level, physico-chemical properties and degradability of the NMI, as well as [route of administration](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%BB%99%E8%8D%AF%E9%80%94%E5%BE%84 "分类:给药途径") and [bioavailability](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%94%9F%E7%89%A9%E5%88%A9%E7%94%A8%E5%BA%A6 "分类:生物利用度"), clinical conditions and target population for the medicinal product. If a need for additional data is identified, the primary source should be existing toxicological data that can be used to derive an Acceptable Level (AL). This can be based on existing impurity-specific data from the literature or data from an adequate surrogate molecule supported by read-across. The AL method estimates a product-specific safe level of exposure to impurities. If an AL above the DS or DP [specification](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%B4%A8%E9%87%8F%E6%A0%87%E5%87%86 "分类:质量标准") limit of an NMI can be established, the level of impurity can be considered toxicologically qualified. The AL method is based on the Permitted Daily Exposure ([PDE](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:PDE "分类:PDE")) methodology described in [ICH Q3C](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3C_R9_Guideline_on_impurities_guideline_for_residual_solvents_20240405 "EMA ICH Q3C R9 Guideline on impurities guideline for residual solvents 20240405"), but also considers bioavailability, read-across data and product-specific information. It involves selecting a point of departure (PoD) from toxicological studies and applying assessment factors (AFs).

是否需要补充数据取决于关注程度。对NMI的关注程度受多种因素影响，包括摄入水平、NMI的理化性质和降解性、给药途径和生物利用度、药品的临床条件和目标人群等。如果确定需要补充数据，首要来源应是现有毒理学数据，可用于推导可接受水平（AL）。这可以基于文献中已有的特定杂质数据，或通过合理的替代分子数据结合读穿（read-across）方法获得。AL方法用于估算产品特定的杂质暴露安全水平。如果能够建立高于原料药或制剂规格限度的NMI的AL，则可认为该杂质水平在毒理学上已获得界定。AL方法基于ICH Q3C中描述的允许日暴露量（PDE）方法，同时考虑生物利用度、读穿数据和产品特定信息。该方法涉及从毒理学研究中选择起始点（PoD）并应用评估因子（AFs）。

In the absence of adequate data to derive an AL, New Approach Methodologies (NAM) can be employed, involving characterisation of chemical properties and computational toxicology tools. Combining evidence from multiple in silico tools or in vitro studies can fill knowledge gaps, with expert opinions complementing the risk assessment.

在缺乏足够数据推导AL的情况下，可以采用新方法学（NAM），包括化学特性表征和计算毒理学工具。结合多个计算机模拟工具或体外研究的证据可以填补知识空白，专家意见可进一步补充风险评估。

Only when alternative methods fail to provide sufficient information to establish the safety of an [impurity](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8 "分类:杂质") at the proposed specification limit, and with appropriate scientific justification, should in vivo studies be considered. In this situation, this reflection paper provides specific recommendations for the design of new in vivo studies for qualification of NMIs.

只有在替代方法无法提供足够信息以确认杂质在拟定规格限下的安全性，并有适当科学依据的情况下，才应考虑体内研究。在这种情况下，本思考性文件为开展新的体内研究以界定NMI提供了具体建议。

Impurities in [investigational medicinal products](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%AF%95%E9%AA%8C%E7%94%A8%E8%8D%AF%E5%93%81 "分类:试验用药品") should be evaluated according to [ICH M3(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_M3_\(R2\)_Non-clinical_safety_studies_for_the_conduct_of_human_clinical_trials_for_pharmaceuticals "EMA ICH M3 (R2) Non-clinical safety studies for the conduct of human clinical trials for pharmaceuticals"). When there is a need for additional safety data, the principles in this reflection paper can complement the approaches described in ICH M3(R2) for evaluating the safety of impurities during clinical development.

临床试验用药品中的杂质应根据ICH M3(R2)进行评估。当需要补充安全性数据时，本思考性文件中的原则可补充ICH M3(R2)中所述方法，用于评估临床开发阶段杂质的安全性。

In summary, when impurity-specific safety information for an NMI is recommended, alternative strategies to gathering this information may be followed, including the use of existing toxicological data, read-across, threshold of toxicological concern ([TTC](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:TTC "分类:TTC")), computational and in vitro approaches. This information can be used in an integrated risk assessment. A weight-of-evidence (WoE) approach that includes all aspects that determine the level of concern, could be sufficient to decide that the NMI can be considered safe at the specified level.

总之，当建议对NMI提供杂质特异性安全性信息时，可采用多种替代策略获取该信息，包括使用现有毒理学数据、读穿方法、毒理学关注阈值（TTC）、计算和体外方法。这些信息可用于综合风险评估。一种包含所有决定关注程度因素的证据权重（WoE）方法可能足以判断NMI在规定水平下是安全的。

# 2. Introduction

2. 引言

The [ICH Q3A(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3A_\(R2\)_Impurities_in_new_drug_substances "EMA ICH Q3A (R2) Impurities in new drug substances") and [Q3B(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3B_\(R2\)_Impurities_in_new_drug_products "EMA ICH Q3B (R2) Impurities in new drug products") guidelines provide a framework for qualifying Non-Mutagenic Impurities (NMI) in DSs and DPs, including reporting, identification and qualification thresholds (QT) for impurities. These guidelines define qualification as "the process of acquiring and evaluating data that establishes the biological safety of an individual impurity or a given [impurity profile](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8%E8%B0%B1 "分类:杂质谱") at the level(s) specified." They also state that "the applicant should provide a rationale for establishing impurity [acceptance criteria](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%8E%A5%E5%8F%97%E6%A0%87%E5%87%86 "分类:接受标准") that includes safety considerations." According to ICH Q3A and Q3B, no further toxicological data is necessary to qualify specified impurity levels, as included in the DS or DP [specifications](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%B4%A8%E9%87%8F%E6%A0%87%E5%87%86 "分类:质量标准"), which are below the qualification thresholds. Furthermore, impurity levels can also be qualified when present in the DS/DP used in the non-clinical repeat dose toxicology studies at levels that do not impact the outcome of the toxicological assessment of DS/DP. In addition to ICH Q3A and Q3B, other impurity specific guidelines are in place such as [ICH M7(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_M7_R2_Guideline_on_assessment_and_control_of_DNA_reactive_mutagenic_impurities_in_pharmaceuticals_to_limit_potential_carcinogenic_risk_20230718 "EMA ICH M7 R2 Guideline on assessment and control of DNA reactive mutagenic impurities in pharmaceuticals to limit potential carcinogenic risk 20230718"), [Q3D](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3D_Elemental_impurities_Adopted_20190328 "EMA ICH Q3D Elemental impurities Adopted 20190328") and [Q3C](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3C_R9_Guideline_on_impurities_guideline_for_residual_solvents_20240405 "EMA ICH Q3C R9 Guideline on impurities guideline for residual solvents 20240405") (see Section 4 for expanded list), which address DNA reactive (mutagenic) [impurities](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8 "分类:杂质"), [elemental impurities](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E5%85%83%E7%B4%A0%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8 "分类:元素杂质") and [residual solvents](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%AE%8B%E7%95%99%E6%BA%B6%E5%89%82 "分类:残留溶剂"), respectively. However, overall, these guidelines offer limited guidance on the process of qualification of newly identified or elevated impurity levels above existing ICH Q3A or Q3B impurity QTs that are identified after non-clinical [toxicology](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%AF%92%E7%90%86%E5%AD%A6 "分类:毒理学") studies for the [API](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E5%8E%9F%E6%96%99%E8%8D%AF "分类:原料药") are complete, beyond the conduct of additional in vivo general toxicology studies. This reflection paper aims to provide recommendations to complement the current approaches in ICH guidelines, specifically to provide recommendations on alternative methods to additional in vivo toxicology testing with NMIs.

ICH Q3A(R2)和Q3B(R2)指南为原料药和制剂中NMI的界定提供了框架，包括杂质的报告、鉴别和界定阈值（QT）。这些指南将界定定义为“获取和评估数据以确立特定杂质或给定杂质谱在规定水平下的生物安全性的过程”。指南还指出，“申请人应提供建立杂质可接受标准的合理依据，包括安全性考虑。”根据ICH Q3A和Q3B，当原料药或制剂规格中包含的特定杂质水平低于界定阈值时，无需进一步毒理学数据即可界定这些杂质水平。此外，当原料药/制剂在非临床重复给药毒理学研究中使用的原料药/制剂中杂质水平未影响原料药/制剂毒理学评估结果时，也可视为已界定杂质水平。除ICH Q3A和Q3B外，还有其他针对特定杂质的指南，如ICH M7(R2)、Q3D和Q3C（详见第4节），分别涉及DNA反应性（致突变性）杂质、元素杂质和残留溶剂。然而总体而言，这些指南对在原料药非临床毒理学研究完成后识别出的新杂质或高于现有ICH Q3A或Q3B杂质界定阈值的杂质的界定过程提供的指导有限，除进行额外的一般体内毒理学研究外。本思考性文件旨在为ICH指南中现有方法提供补充建议，特别是提供替代额外体内毒理学测试的NMI方法建议。

The [ICH Q3A](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3A_\(R2\)_Impurities_in_new_drug_substances "EMA ICH Q3A (R2) Impurities in new drug substances")/[Q3B](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3B_\(R2\)_Impurities_in_new_drug_products "EMA ICH Q3B (R2) Impurities in new drug products") guidelines recommend that additional safety testing should be considered when higher levels or new impurities, that cannot be qualified through existing non-clinical data, trigger the need for qualification. As a result, dedicated animal studies have often been conducted with the aim of qualifying these impurities, however, these studies are often designed using impurity-spiked API-batches instead of testing the neat impurity, which has several limitations.

ICH Q3A/Q3B指南建议，当更高水平或新杂质无法通过现有非临床数据界定且触发界定需求时，应考虑额外的安全性测试。因此，通常会开展专门的动物研究以界定这些杂质，但这些研究通常使用掺入杂质的原料药批次而非纯杂质进行测试，存在若干局限性。

In the impurity-spiked DS studies, the biological safety of the DP is established as a whole based on its specific impurity profile, rather than characterising the safety of the individual impurities. When toxicity is observed, it is typically not possible to determine whether it is attributable to the API or to one or more of the impurities present in the DP batch. Impurities are typically also present at much lower concentrations than the API, adding further complexity in attributing any observed effects specifically to the impurity. Thus, No Observed Adverse Effect Level ([NOAEL](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:NOAEL "分类:NOAEL")) derived from such studies is most likely reflective of the API's safety profile rather than that of the impurity (Graham et al., 2021). While these studies may be considered valid in terms of design, they do not yield relevant information regarding the biological safety of the impurity. This undermines the scientific justification for conducting such studies and conflicts with Directive 2010/63/EU on the protection of animals used for scientific purposes, which discourages animal testing when it is unlikely to produce meaningful data. In fact, a survey amongst stakeholders reviewing in vivo studies on 467 impurities found no cases where toxic impurities were identified. In 98.7% of these studies, the impurity was present— either spiked or unspiked —at low levels in the DS (Slikkerveer et al., 2024), further highlighting the limited value of this approach. Another key observation when non-[clinical studies](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E4%B8%B4%E5%BA%8A%E7%A0%94%E7%A9%B6 "分类:临床研究") on the DS is used for qualification of impurities is that allometric scaling is often not considered and the safety evaluation does thus not consider the biological differences between humans and animal species. In some instances, this leads to an in vivo study not being accepted by regulators to support the impurity safety evaluation as the impurity levels in the DS non-clinical study are too low when also applying corrections for allometric scaling to the NOAEL.

在原料药掺杂杂质的研究中，制剂的整体生物安全性是基于其特定杂质谱建立的，而非对单个杂质的安全性进行表征。当观察到毒性时，通常无法确定其是由原料药引起还是由制剂批次中存在的一个或多个杂质引起。杂质通常以远低于原料药的浓度存在，这进一步增加了将观察到的效应归因于杂质的复杂性。因此，从此类研究中得出的未见不良反应剂量水平（NOAEL）很可能反映的是原料药的安全性特征，而非杂质的安全性特征（Graham等，2021）。尽管这些研究在设计上可能被认为是有效的，但它们并未提供有关杂质生物安全性的相关信息。这削弱了开展此类研究的科学依据，并与《欧盟2010/63号指令》关于保护用于科学目的的动物的规定相冲突，该指令不鼓励在不太可能产生有意义数据的情况下进行动物实验。事实上，一项对审查了467种杂质体内研究的利益相关方的调查显示，未发现任何识别出有毒杂质的案例。在98.7%的这些研究中，原料药中杂质含量（无论是掺入还是未掺入）均处于低水平（Slikkerveer等，2024），进一步凸显了这种方法的有限价值。另一个关键观察结果是，当使用原料药的非临床研究用于杂质界定时，常常未考虑异速缩放，因此安全性评估未考虑人类与动物物种之间的生物学差异。在某些情况下，这导致监管机构不接受体内研究以支持杂质安全性评估，因为当对NOAEL进行异速缩放校正后，原料药非临床研究中的杂质水平过低。

The approach of using impurity-spiked DS batches also stands in contrasts to the compound-specific approaches adopted for mutagenic impurities, elemental impurities and solvents, as well as the approach under development for extractables and leachables. These guidelines emphasise the importance of evaluating the safety of individual impurities, rather than relying solely on the overall impurity profile of the DS or DP.

采用杂质加标原料药批次的方法也与针对致突变杂质、元素杂质和溶剂所采用的化合物特异性方法，以及针对溶出物和析出物正在开发中的方法形成对比。这些指南强调了评估单个杂质安全性的重要性，而不是仅仅依赖原料药或制剂的整体杂质谱。

In cases where impurities have not been previously qualified in safety studies (i.e. novel impurities), or when higher levels of impurities need to be qualified (that were previously qualified at a lower level), the use of alternative, non-animal methodologies are recommended. Only in rare instances — where a residual safety concern cannot be resolved through other means — should the conduct of an animal study be considered. In such cases, a study with the neat impurity is more likely to provide relevant information on the impurity's safety profile due to the reasons outlined above.

对于此前未在安全研究中被确认的杂质（即新型杂质），或需要对更高水平的杂质进行确认（此前已在较低水平下被确认）的情况，建议采用替代的非动物方法。只有在极少数情况下 - 即通过其他方式无法解决残留安全问题时 - 才应考虑进行动物研究。在这种情况下，由于上述原因，使用纯杂质进行的研究更有可能提供有关该杂质安全谱的相关信息。

This reflection paper addresses the qualification of NMI from a safety perspective when impurity levels included in the DS or DP [specification](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%B4%A8%E9%87%8F%E6%A0%87%E5%87%86 "分类:质量标准") exceed the QTs in ICH Q3A and Q3B. It does not address the acceptability of impurity levels from a quality perspective.

本思考性文件从安全角度讨论了当原料药或制剂质量标准中包含的杂质水平超过ICH Q3A和Q3B中规定的鉴定阈值（QT）时，如何对非致突变杂质（NMI）进行确认。本文件不涉及从质量角度评估杂质水平的可接受性。

# 3. Scope

3. 适用范围

This reflection paper outlines the current thinking on recommended approaches for evaluating the safety of NMIs. The principles and methodologies discussed are intended to support the qualification of new or elevated [impurity](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8 "分类:杂质") levels, which are specified at levels that exceed existing ICH Q3A/Q3B QTs, particularly when such impurities are identified after completion of non-clinical toxicology studies. These new or elevated impurity levels may result from any type of DS or DP related changes, such as changes in manufacturing processes. The reflection paper does not reflect further on the ALARP principle (As Low As Reasonably Possible) for the limits of impurities, but specification levels should generally be based on batch data and not only be guided by toxicologically qualified levels. In addition, this paper complements the approaches described in [ICH M3(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_M3_\(R2\)_Non-clinical_safety_studies_for_the_conduct_of_human_clinical_trials_for_pharmaceuticals "EMA ICH M3 (R2) Non-clinical safety studies for the conduct of human clinical trials for pharmaceuticals") for evaluating the safety of impurities during clinical development. Its aim is to supplement existing guidelines —specifically [ICH Q3A(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3A_\(R2\)_Impurities_in_new_drug_substances "EMA ICH Q3A (R2) Impurities in new drug substances") and [Q3B(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3B_\(R2\)_Impurities_in_new_drug_products "EMA ICH Q3B (R2) Impurities in new drug products")— by proposing alternative, non-animal strategies for the qualification of NMIs. These approaches may offer more impurity-specific information than traditional in vivo studies. It also acknowledges that the level of concern for impurities may vary depending on multiple factors, which in turn determine the extent of data needed, ranging from no additional data to impurity-specific experimental studies.

本思考性文件概述了当前关于评估非致突变杂质（NMI）安全性的推荐方法的观点。所讨论的原则和方法旨在支持对新的或升高的杂质水平进行确认，这些杂质水平被规定在超过现有ICH Q3A/Q3B鉴定阈值（QT）的水平，特别是当这些杂质在非临床毒理学研究完成后被识别时。这些新的或升高的杂质水平可能源于任何类型的原料药或制剂相关变更，例如生产工艺的变更。本思考性文件未进一步讨论杂质限度的“尽可能低原则”（ALARP），但质量标准水平通常应基于批次数据，而不仅仅由毒理学确认的水平决定。此外，本文补充了ICH M3(R2)中描述的在临床开发期间评估杂质安全性的方法。其目的是通过提出替代的非动物策略来补充现有指南 - 特别是ICH Q3A(R2)和Q3B(R2) - 用于对非致突变杂质（NMI）进行确认。这些方法可能比传统的体内研究提供更多的杂质特异性信息。同时，本文也承认，对杂质的关注程度可能因多种因素而异，进而决定所需数据的范围，从无需额外数据到杂质特异性实验研究不等。

The scope of this reflection paper is limited to NMIs in chemically synthesised pharmaceuticals. It does not address impurities in Advanced Therapy Medicinal Products ([ATMP](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E5%85%88%E8%BF%9B%E7%96%97%E6%B3%95 "分类:先进疗法")), in [herbal medicinal products](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%A4%8D%E7%89%A9%E8%8D%AF "分类:植物药"), and in [biological](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%94%9F%E7%89%A9%E5%88%B6%E5%93%81 "分类:生物制品") and biotechnologically derived pharmaceuticals. However, for combination products containing both chemically synthesised and biotechnologically derived components (e.g. antibody drug conjugates) the principles outlined in this paper may be considered where appropriate.

本思考性文件的适用范围限于化学合成药品中的非致突变杂质（NMI）。本文件不涉及先进治疗药物产品（ATMP）、草药药品、生物和生物技术来源药品中的杂质。然而，对于含有化学合成和生物技术来源成分的组合产品（例如抗体药物偶联物），在适当的情况下可考虑应用本文所述的原则。

# 5. Key considerations

5. 关键考虑因素

## 5.1. General outline for risk assessment of NMIs

5.1. 非致突变杂质（NMI）风险评估的一般框架

The principles described in this reflection paper may be considered for the toxicological qualification of identified NMIs when the following conditions are met:

当满足以下条件时，可考虑采用本思考性文件中描述的原则对已识别的非致突变杂质（NMI）进行毒理学确认：

a) the specified level of the impurity in the DS or DP specification exceeds the QT as defined in [ICH Q3A(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3A_\(R2\)_Impurities_in_new_drug_substances "EMA ICH Q3A (R2) Impurities in new drug substances") and [Q3B(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3B_\(R2\)_Impurities_in_new_drug_products "EMA ICH Q3B (R2) Impurities in new drug products");

a) 原料药或制剂质量标准中规定的杂质水平超过了ICH Q3A(R2)和Q3B(R2)中定义的鉴定阈值（QT）；

b) existing non-clinical studies with the DS do not adequately address the impurity at the identified/specified levels (e.g. the impurity was not detected or was present only at low concentrations).

b) 原料药现有的非临床研究未能充分评估在已识别/规定水平下的杂质（例如，杂质未被检测到或仅以低浓度存在）。

The principles of this reflection paper may also be applicable when the NMI is not covered by other impurity-specific guidelines, as listed in Section 4.

当非致突变杂质（NMI）未被第4节中列出的其他杂质特异性指南涵盖时，也可适用本思考性文件的原则。

The general strategy for risk assessment of NMIs is illustrated in the flow chart in Figure 1.

NMI风险评估的一般策略如图1中的流程图所示。

The flow chart is structured into focused areas of evaluation to support a stepwise assessment of NMIs. This approach enables the use of a more limited data package when specific information—such as non-clinical or clinical metabolite levels, or an assessment of [API](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E5%8E%9F%E6%96%99%E8%8D%AF "分类:原料药")-derived impurities —is available. Each step in the flow chart corresponds to a dedicated section in the reflection paper.

流程图分为若干重点评估领域，以支持对NMI的分步评估。当有特定信息（例如非临床或临床代谢物水平，或对原料药来源杂质的评估）可用时，该方法允许使用较为有限的数据包。流程图中的每一步骤均对应思考性文件中的专门章节。

Impurities that are also observed as metabolites at sufficient levels in animals and/or humans are discussed in Section 5.2. In such cases, the impurity may be considered qualified without the need for additional toxicological data. Section 5.3.2. addresses API-derived impurities that do not introduce new [structural alerts](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%AD%A6%E7%A4%BA%E7%BB%93%E6%9E%84 "分类:警示结构") compared to the API. These are generally considered to pose low toxicological concern and are assumed to be covered by the existing toxicological studies of the DS.

在动物和/或人类中以足够水平观察到的、同时也是代谢物的杂质将在第5.2节中讨论。在这种情况下，杂质可被认为已获得认可，无需额外的毒理学数据。第5.3.2节涉及与原料药相比未引入新结构警示的原料药来源杂质。这些杂质通常被认为毒理学担忧较低，并假定其已被原料药现有毒理学研究覆盖。

For all other impurities that do not fall within the categories described in Sections 5.2. and 5.3.2., the level of toxicological concern should be assessed as outlined in Section 5.4. This evaluation could consider factors such as the intended patient population, [route of administration](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%BB%99%E8%8D%AF%E9%80%94%E5%BE%84 "分类:给药途径"), and duration of treatment. If the outcome of this assessment indicates that the identified level of the NMI does not pose a toxicological concern, no further qualification is considered necessary. In such cases, the impurity level may be considered qualified.

对于不属于第5.2节中所述类别的所有其他杂质和5.3.2节所述类别，应按照第5.4节所述评估其毒理学担忧水平。该评估可考虑诸如预期患者人群、给药途径和治疗持续时间等因素。如果该评估的结果表明NMI的已识别水平不会造成毒理学担忧，则无需进一步的认可。在这种情况下，杂质水平可被认为已获得认可。

If the level of toxicological concern warrants further evaluation, the assessment should begin with a literature search for existing impurity-specific in vitro or in vivo data relevant for a toxicological risk assessment. Where in vivo data is available, an Acceptable Level (AL) should be derived following the principles outlined in Section 5.5. In the absence of impurity-specific data, it should be determined whether suitable surrogate compounds with relevant toxicological data are available. If such surrogates are identified, read across should be conducted following the principles outlined in Section 5.3., and in vivo data for the surrogate may be used to derive an AL. If the level of the NMI is below the derived AL, the impurity is considered toxicologically qualified. If impurity level exceeds the AL, the level of concern should be re-evaluated (see Section 5.4.1.), taking into consideration the toxicological properties of the impurity and the extent to which the AL is exceeded.

如果毒理学担忧水平需要进一步评估，则评估应首先进行文献检索，寻找与杂质相关的现有体外或体内毒理风险评估数据。如果存在体内数据，应根据第5.5节所述原则推导出可接受水平（AL）。在缺乏杂质特异性数据的情况下，应确定是否存在具有相关毒理学数据的合适替代化合物。如果识别到此类替代物，则应按照第5.3节所述原则进行交叉读取，并可使用替代物的体内数据推导出AL。如果NMI的水平低于推导出的AL，则认为杂质在毒理学上已获得认可。如果杂质水平超过AL，则应重新评估担忧水平（见第5.4.1节），并考虑杂质的毒理学特性以及超过AL的程度。

In case impurity-specific data or data from a surrogate compound cannot be identified, New Approach Methodologies (NAMs) described in Section 5.6. may be applied to qualify the impurity. These include computational toxicology approaches (see Section 5.6.1.) e.g. using (Q)SAR models as well as in vitro approaches (see Section 5.6.2.) to evaluate the toxicological hazard of the impurity. NAM data may be combined in an integrated assessment approach, e.g. by building data into established AOPs for specific endpoints. Based on the predicted toxicity and the associated uncertainties of the computational models, a level of concern assessment should be conducted. If the potential risk is deemed acceptable, no further data are considered necessary. However, if the assessment indicates a potentially unacceptable risk at the maximum daily intake of the [impurity](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8 "分类:杂质"), the impurity level should be reduced, or additional toxicological data should be generated.

如果无法识别杂质特异性数据或替代化合物数据，则可应用第5.6节中描述的新方法学（NAMs）以认可杂质。这些方法包括计算毒理学方法（见第5.6.1节），例如使用（Q）SAR模型，以及体外方法（见第5.6.2节）以评估杂质的毒理学危害。NAM数据可在综合评估方法中结合使用，例如通过将数据构建到特定终点的已建立AOP中。基于预测的毒性和计算模型的相关不确定性，应进行担忧水平评估。如果潜在风险被认为可接受，则无需进一步数据。然而，如果评估表明在杂质最大每日摄入量下存在潜在不可接受的风险，则应降低杂质水平，或生成额外的毒理学数据。

In such cases, in vitro approaches may be considered to further characterise the hazard of the impurity (see Section 5.6.2.). Only in exceptional circumstances—where none of the aforementioned methods have resulted in sufficient data to support qualification—should in vivo studies be considered to address remaining data gaps (see Section 5.7.).

在这种情况下，可考虑使用体外方法进一步表征杂质的危害（见第5.6.2节）。只有在极少数情况下 - 即上述方法均未产生足够数据以支持认可时 - 才应考虑进行体内研究以填补剩余的数据缺口（见第5.7节）。

## 5.2. Metabolites

5.2 代谢物

In line with [ICH Q3A(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3A_\(R2\)_Impurities_in_new_drug_substances "EMA ICH Q3A (R2) Impurities in new drug substances") and [Q3B(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3B_\(R2\)_Impurities_in_new_drug_products "EMA ICH Q3B (R2) Impurities in new drug products"), "impurities that are also significant metabolites present in animal and/or human studies are considered qualified". The term "significant metabolite" should not be understood in the context of [ICH M3(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_M3_\(R2\)_Non-clinical_safety_studies_for_the_conduct_of_human_clinical_trials_for_pharmaceuticals "EMA ICH M3 (R2) Non-clinical safety studies for the conduct of human clinical trials for pharmaceuticals"), where it is defined as the threshold of 10% at which additional non-clinical characterisation of a metabolite is warranted. For the qualification of an NMI, existing non-clinical and [clinical studies](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E4%B8%B4%E5%BA%8A%E7%A0%94%E7%A9%B6 "分类:临床研究") with the DS, in which sufficient exposure levels of metabolite have been demonstrated and the metabolite is chemically identical to the NMI, even when below 10% of the DS, are considered relevant to address the safety profile of the impurity. In this case the term "significant metabolite" is related to the extent of exposure level of the metabolite (EM) in the (non-) clinical studies with the DS relative to the anticipated exposure level of the NMI (EI) when being exposed to the maximum daily dose (MDD) of the medicinal product. As shown in the flow chart (Figure 1), the ratio (EM/EI) between the exposure to the metabolite and the anticipated exposure to the impurity should be established.

根据ICH Q3A(R2)和Q3B(R2)，"在动物和/或人体研究中也作为重要代谢物出现的杂质被视为已获得认可"。术语"重要代谢物"不应理解为ICH M3(R2)中的定义，其中将其定义为需要对代谢物进行额外非临床表征的10%阈值。对于NMI的认可，现有非临床和临床研究中已证明代谢物暴露水平足够，并且代谢物在化学上与NMI相同，即使低于原料药的10%，也被认为与杂质安全性特征相关。在这种情况下，术语"重要代谢物"是指在原料药的（非）临床研究中代谢物的暴露水平（EM）相对于药物产品最大每日剂量（MDD）下NMI预期暴露水平（EI）的程度。如流程图（图1）所示，应建立代谢物暴露量与NMI预期暴露量之间的比率（EM/EI）。

If sufficient levels of metabolite have been demonstrated and the metabolite is chemically identical to the impurity, the NMI can usually be qualified using the existing (non-)clinical studies with the DS to address the safety profile of the impurity.

如果已证明代谢物具有足够水平并且代谢物在化学上与杂质相同，则通常可以使用现有（非）临床研究中的原料药数据来认可NMI并评估杂质的安全性特征。

While available animal metabolism data is normally used for qualifying an impurity, metabolism data in humans can also be used if the NMI is detected as a metabolite in humans. Generally, exposure levels of the metabolite should be reported based on metabolism data obtained in plasma. However, if scientifically justified, urinary metabolism data may also be acceptable to support the qualification of an impurity. The daily dose of an impurity is calculated as the percentage in the DP [specification](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%B4%A8%E9%87%8F%E6%A0%87%E5%87%86 "分类:质量标准") relative to the MDD. However, this does not consider the [ADME](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:ADME "分类:ADME") properties to inform on the actual systemic exposure levels achieved for the impurity, complicating a direct comparison between levels of impurities and plasma metabolites for establishing the exposure margin. Weidolf et al. (2020) have proposed a pragmatic method for estimating the intrinsic exposure level of an impurity to compare with the observed level of plasma metabolite, thus establishing the EM/EI ratio. They propose to calculate the maximum theoretical concentration (MTC) achieved for the impurity (also referred to as EI) and to compare this with the maximum observed concentration (MOC) of the metabolite (also referred to as EM).

虽然通常使用现有的动物代谢数据来认可杂质，但如果在人体中检测到NMI作为代谢物，则也可以使用人体代谢数据。通常，代谢物的暴露水平应基于血浆中获得的代谢数据进行报告。然而，如果有科学依据，尿液代谢数据也可用于支持杂质的界定。杂质的日剂量计算为DP质量标准中相对于MDD的百分比。然而，这未考虑杂质的ADME特性，无法反映杂质实际达到的全身暴露水平，从而难以直接比较杂质水平与血浆代谢物水平以确定暴露余量。Weidolf等人（2020）提出了一种实用的方法来估算杂质的内在暴露水平，并与观察到的血浆代谢物水平进行比较，从而建立EM/EI比值。他们建议计算杂质达到的最大理论浓度（MTC）（也称为EI），并将其与代谢物的最大观察浓度（MOC）（也称为EM）进行比较。

The MOC (or EM) of the metabolite is taken from an animal toxicity study or a relevant [clinical study](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E4%B8%B4%E5%BA%8A%E7%A0%94%E7%A9%B6 "分类:临床研究"). The average plasma concentration in the relevant dose group of animals or patients/volunteers would be considered to represent the MOC. Thus, EM = average plasma concentration of the metabolite in animals or patients.

代谢物的MOC（或EM）取自动物毒性研究或相关临床研究。在动物或患者/志愿者的相关剂量组中的平均血浆浓度将被视为MOC。因此，EM = 代谢物在动物或人体内的平均血浆浓度。

When calculating the MTC (or EI), worst case assumptions are considered, such as complete [bioavailability](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%94%9F%E7%89%A9%E5%88%A9%E7%94%A8%E5%BA%A6 "分类:生物利用度") with no plasma-protein binding, no distribution into blood cells or other tissues, and no elimination. It is proposed to use the extracellular fluid (ECF) of the selected species as the minimal [volume of distribution](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%A1%A8%E8%A7%82%E5%88%86%E5%B8%83%E5%AE%B9%E7%A7%AF "分类:表观分布容积") for estimating the systemic concentration of the impurity. In the paper by Weidolf et al. (2020), MTC is established based on the daily exposure of the impurity (mg/day), which is calculated using the MDD and the impurity specification level, as well as an average rat ECF volume of 80.4 ml. Alternatively, if calculating the MTC to compare with human metabolism data, a human ECF volume of 14 L can be used (Tobias, 2025). Thus, EI = MDD x %specification levelimpurity / ECF, where ECF in humans and rats is 14 L and 80.4 mL, respectively.

在计算MTC（或EI）时，应考虑最不利的情况，例如完全生物利用度、无血浆蛋白结合、无分布至血细胞或其他组织以及无消除。建议使用所选物种的细胞外液（ECF）作为估算杂质系统浓度的最小分布容积。在Weidolf等人（2020）的文章中，MTC是基于杂质的每日暴露量（mg/天）建立的，该暴露量使用MDD和杂质质量标准水平计算得出，并结合了大鼠平均ECF体积80.4 ml。或者，如果计算MTC以与人体代谢数据进行比较，可以使用14 L的人体ECF体积（Tobias，2025）。因此，EI = MDD × 杂质质量标准水平% / ECF，其中人体和大鼠的ECF分别为14 L和80.4 mL。

For a metabolite/impurity of low concern, no further non-clinical data is needed to qualify the impurity when unity is reached (EM/EI ≥ 1). However, when potential toxicity of the metabolite/impurity is of concern, a larger EM/EI needs to be considered to ensure that unacceptable exposure due to the additional presence of the substance as a drug-related impurity is not achieved. A metabolite is considered of low concern when it has been identified at proportionate levels in animals and humans and it is not a human-specific or significant human metabolite (i.e. ≤ 10%). In case of a significant human metabolite, a disproportionate metabolite or a human-specific metabolite, it should be sufficiently characterised to justify that the metabolite does not significantly add to the toxicity profile of the parent before it can be considered of low concern. If exacerbated toxicities of the metabolite cannot be ruled out or if no toxicological data is available for the metabolite, an EM/EI ≥ 10 should be applied.

对于低关注度的代谢物/杂质，当达到等值时（EM/EI ≥ 1），无需进一步的非临床数据来界定该杂质。然而，当代谢物/杂质的潜在毒性值得关注时，需要考虑更大的EM/EI比值，以确保由于该物质作为药物相关杂质的额外存在而不会导致不可接受的暴露。当代谢物已在动物和人体中以成比例的水平被鉴定，并且不是人体特异性或重要的人体代谢物（即 ≤ 10%）时，则认为其为低关注度代谢物。对于重要的人体代谢物、不成比例的代谢物或人体特异性代谢物，应充分表征其以证明该代谢物不会显著增加母体化合物的毒性特征，之后才可认为其为低关注度代谢物。如果无法排除代谢物的增强毒性，或代谢物没有毒理学数据，则应采用EM/EI ≥ 10的标准。

## 5.3. Read across

5.3. 交叉读取

If no sufficiently robust in vitro or in vivo toxicity data can be identified on the [impurity](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8 "分类:杂质") itself, it is possible to perform read-across to one or more surrogate compounds for which robust in vitro or in vivo toxicity data is available. The single surrogate approach or the grouping approach can be employed to identify qualitative or quantitative in vitro or in vivo toxicity data for chemically similar compounds that can be used to qualify the impurity at the specified level. API-related impurities may also be assessed in terms of similarity between the [API](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E5%8E%9F%E6%96%99%E8%8D%AF "分类:原料药") and the impurity to determine if the non-clinical experimental data for the DS can be used to qualify the impurity.

如果无法获得关于杂质本身的足够可靠的体外或体内毒性数据，则可以将数据交叉读取至一个或多个具有可靠体外或体内毒性数据的替代化合物。可以采用单一替代方法或分组方法来识别化学结构相似化合物的定性或定量体外或体内毒性数据，以用于在规定水平上界定杂质。原料药相关杂质也可根据原料药与杂质之间的相似性进行评估，以确定原料药的非临床实验数据是否可用于界定杂质。

### 5.3.1. Surrogate approach

5.3.1. 替代方法

In a read-across framework, three different steps need to be considered: 1) Chemical similarity assessment between target and surrogate molecule, 2) Toxicological assessment of data and 3) Establishing an AL.

在交叉读取框架中，需要考虑三个不同的步骤：1）目标分子与替代分子之间的化学相似性评估，2）毒理学数据评估，3）建立AL。

Chemical similarity assessment between target and surrogate molecule

目标分子与替代分子之间的化学相似性评估

When performing read-across to a surrogate compound, firstly, the impurity should be characterised in terms of chemical-structural and physicochemical properties as well as pharmacokinetic ([PK](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%8D%AF%E4%BB%A3%E5%8A%A8%E5%8A%9B%E5%AD%A6 "分类:药代动力学")) properties when available. Comparability based on physicochemical properties or PK properties, where available, should be discussed.

在将数据交叉读取至替代化合物时，首先应从化学结构和理化性质以及可用的药代动力学（PK）特性方面对杂质进行表征。应讨论基于理化性质或PK性质的可比性（如适用）。

Relevant toxicophores that are present in the impurity should be identified, where a toxicophore is defined as a chemical structure or part of a structure that is related to the toxic properties of the impurity. This can include both pharmacologically active and non-active moieties of the impurity. Physicochemical properties (such as polarity, solubility, lipophilicity, ionisability, and molecular weight), as well as PK properties (such as bioavailability, distribution, metabolism, and excretion) should be considered, e.g. from databases or based on predictions using computational tools. When in silico tools are used, it should be justified that these are fit-for-purpose (see section 5.6.1.). Also, considerations regarding biological plausibility (e.g. mechanism/mode of action) may be included in the assessment.

应识别杂质中存在的相关毒理基团，毒理基团定义为与杂质毒性特性相关的化学结构或结构的一部分。这可以包括杂质的药理活性和非活性部分。应考虑理化性质（如极性、溶解度、亲脂性、离子化能力和分子量）以及PK性质（如生物利用度、分布、代谢和排泄），例如来自数据库或基于计算工具预测的结果。当使用计算机预测工具时，应证明其适合预期用途（见第5.6.1节）。此外，评估中还可包括对生物学合理性的考虑（例如机制/作用模式）。

Surrogate compounds, for which robust data is available, should be identified based on similarities to the NMI. The presence of the identified toxicophores of the impurity should be demonstrated in the surrogate compounds, and further, the presence of other functional groups – especially those spatially close to the toxicophore, which could potentially affect the [biological activity](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%94%9F%E7%89%A9%E5%AD%A6%E6%B4%BB%E6%80%A7 "分类:生物学活性"), should be identified. Multi-metric approaches (including Cosine, Tanimoto, and/or other similarity metrics), combined with the use of well-established molecular descriptors when appropriate, can be applied to assess molecular similarity (Sheridan et al., 2002 and Willett et al., 1998). The choice of adequate surrogate(s) should be justified based on the similarity and uncertainties with the read-across method and the adequacy of the outcome of the assessment should be provided together with the overall outcome of the read-across approach.

应基于与NMI的相似性识别具有可靠数据的替代化合物。应证明替代化合物中存在杂质的已识别毒理基团，并进一步识别其他官能团 - 特别是那些在空间上接近毒理基团、可能影响生物活性的官能团。在适当的情况下，可以应用多指标方法（包括余弦、Tanimoto和/或其他相似性度量）结合使用公认的分子描述符来评估分子相似性（Sheridan等人，2002年；Willett等人，1998年）。应基于相似性及交叉读取方法的不确定性来证明所选替代物的适当性，并应提供评估结果的充分性以及交叉读取方法整体结果。

As detailed in the Computational toxicology section (Section 5.6.1.), different tools for predictions could be used for identifying toxicophores associated with endpoint-specific toxicities, e.g. (Quantitative) Structure–Activity Relationship (Q)SAR, as well as for predicting physicochemical and PK properties. It is acknowledged that the only endpoint in (Q)SAR modeling currently considered regulatory validated is mutagenicity in bacteria, as described in [ICH M7(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_M7_R2_Guideline_on_assessment_and_control_of_DNA_reactive_mutagenic_impurities_in_pharmaceuticals_to_limit_potential_carcinogenic_risk_20230718 "EMA ICH M7 R2 Guideline on assessment and control of DNA reactive mutagenic impurities in pharmaceuticals to limit potential carcinogenic risk 20230718"). Nonetheless, computational tools could be used for identifying toxicophores considered relevant for major targets (liver, kidney, cardiovascular system (CVS), gastrointestinal tract (GIT), skin, CNS and respiratory system) to support the read-across assessment. Further recommendations are given in Section 5.6.1. on the use and outcome reporting of (Q)SAR tools to predict toxicity and identify toxicophores.

如计算毒理学部分（第5.6.1节）所述，可以使用不同的预测工具来识别与终点特异性毒性相关的毒理基团，例如（定量）结构-活性关系（Q）SAR，以及预测理化和PK性质。目前在（Q）SAR建模中，唯一被认为监管验证的终点是细菌致突变性，如ICH M7(R2)中所述。然而，可以使用计算工具来鉴别与主要靶标（肝脏、肾脏、心血管系统（CVS）、胃肠道（GIT）、皮肤、中枢神经系统和呼吸系统）相关的毒理基团，以支持交叉读取评估。第5.6.1节中给出了进一步建议。关于（Q）SAR工具的使用及毒性预测和毒理基团鉴别的结果报告。

Toxicological assessment of data

数据的毒理学评估

When one or more suitable surrogate compounds have been identified, an evaluation of the robustness of the available toxicological data should be performed to conclude whether the data is sufficient to establish an AL or perform a hazard assessment. Toxicity studies covering different endpoints could be used for informing on hazard or potency level, and it should be justified that the studies and endpoints for the surrogate compound are also relevant for establishing an AL or de-risking the [impurity](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8 "分类:杂质").

当已确定一个或多个合适的替代化合物时，应对现有毒理学数据的稳健性进行评估，以判断这些数据是否足以建立可接受限度（AL）或进行危害评估。涵盖不同终点的毒性研究可用于提供危害或毒性强度水平的信息，且应论证替代化合物的研究和终点对于建立AL或降低杂质风险也是相关的。

Establishing an AL

建立可接受限度（AL）

Based on the outcome of the read-across assessment, quantitative data on a surrogate could be used to derive an AL as defined in Section 5.5., while qualitative data could be used to de-risk an impurity as not adding significantly to the toxicity of the DS. If more than one surrogate is used to support a read-across assessment and ALs are calculated for each surrogate, the most conservative value should be used to set the AL for the NMI, unless there is convincing evidence that the impurity is less potent, and a higher AL could be accepted.

根据交叉读取评估的结果，可根据第5.5节中的定义，使用替代化合物的定量数据推导出AL；而定性数据可用于将杂质的风险降低至不显著增加原料药（DS）毒性的程度。如果使用多个替代化合物支持交叉读取评估，并为每个替代化合物计算了AL，则应使用最保守的数值作为NMI的AL，除非有令人信服的证据表明该杂质的毒性较低，可以接受更高的AL。

### 5.3.2. API-like vs. non-API-like impurities

5.3.2. 原料药类与非原料药类杂质

All the degradation-and process**-**related impurities of the API that are structurally similar can be considered API-like and allow for read-across to the non-clinical studies with the DS for the purpose of qualification of the NMI. This may include [degradation products](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E9%99%8D%E8%A7%A3%E4%BA%A7%E7%89%A9 "分类:降解产物") of oxidation or hydrolysis reactions, where [minor changes](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E5%BE%AE%E5%B0%8F%E5%8F%98%E6%9B%B4 "分类:微小变更") to the structure are introduced, and the difference does not affect the overall structure or size of the molecule. Specifically, the majority of chemical functional groups should be preserved in an API-like NMI while only 1-2 groups are changed via e.g. degradation by oxidation or hydrolysis. Physicochemical parameters should not be significantly impacted, and it should be justified that changes would not lead to increased toxic potency compared to the API. Moreover, it should be shown that the new chemical groups do not form a new toxicophore compared to groups present in the API. The term toxicophore is defined in section 5.3.1. Susceptible groups in the API includes e.g. carboxylate esters, amides, or carbamate groups that are prone to hydrolysis and carboxylic acids, carboxylate esters, hydroxyl groups, unsaturated hydrocarbons, amide groups and amine groups that are susceptible to degradation by oxidation (Xiao, 2022). Also, in cases where the impurity is a dimer or trimer of the parent structure, the impurity can be considered API-like if it can be justified that the dimerisation bridge does not introduce a new toxicophore (e.g. empagliflozin sugar dimer) and the impurity will degrade back into the parent structure once it has entered the systemic circulation. If degradation of a dimer is not likely to take place, further qualification of the impurity should be considered in terms of altered physical-chemical properties and [biological activity](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%94%9F%E7%89%A9%E5%AD%A6%E6%B4%BB%E6%80%A7 "分类:生物学活性").

所有与原料药结构相似的降解和工艺相关杂质均可视为原料药类杂质，允许将原料药的非临床研究结果交叉读取用于NMI的确认。这可能包括氧化或水解反应的降解产物，其中结构仅发生微小变更，且这些变化不影响分子的整体结构或大小。具体而言，原料药类NMI应保留大部分化学官能团，仅通过例如氧化或水解降解改变1-2个基团。理化参数不应受到显著影响，且应论证这些变化不会导致与原料药相比毒性强度增加。此外，应证明新引入的化学基团不会形成与原料药中存在的基团不同的新毒理基团。毒理基团的定义见第5.3.1节。原料药中易受影响的基团包括例如易水解的羧酸酯、酰胺或氨基甲酸酯基团，以及易氧化降解的羧酸、羧酸酯、羟基、不饱和烃、酰胺基团和胺基团（Xiao, 2022）。此外，在杂质为母体结构的二聚体或三聚体的情况下，如果可以论证二聚桥不会引入新的毒理基团（例如恩格列净糖二聚体），并且该杂质一旦进入体循环后会降解回母体结构，则该杂质可被视为原料药类杂质。如果二聚体的降解不太可能发生，则应进一步考虑杂质的理化性质和生物活性的改变，进行进一步的确认。

As scoped out in the section on read across and computational toxicology, similarity may be evaluated by using computational predictive tools that identify toxicophores and predict physicochemical and PK properties for the impurity, to compare the properties between the [API](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E5%8E%9F%E6%96%99%E8%8D%AF "分类:原料药") and the impurity. This includes an evaluation of whether new toxicophores are introduced and whether physicochemical parameters are impacted, such as molecular weight, lipophilicity, and water solubility. When it is concluded that no new toxicophores are introduced in the NMI compared to the API, and that physicochemical and PK parameters are not significantly affected and therefore not likely to increase toxic potency compared to the API, the impurity is considered API-like. Thus, no further investigations are recommended, as the toxicological properties of the impurity are covered by existing non-[clinical studies](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E4%B8%B4%E5%BA%8A%E7%A0%94%E7%A9%B6 "分类:临床研究") with the DS.

如在交叉读取和计算毒理学部分所述，可以使用计算预测工具识别毒理基团并预测杂质的理化和药代动力学（PK）特性，以比较原料药与杂质之间的特性来评估相似性。这包括评估是否引入了新的毒理基团，以及理化参数是否受到影响，例如分子量、亲脂性和水溶性。当得出结论认为与原料药相比，NMI未引入新的毒理基团，且理化和PK参数未受到显著影响，因此不太可能导致毒性强度增加时，则该杂质被视为原料药类杂质。因此，不建议进一步研究，因为杂质的毒理特性已被现有原料药的非临床研究覆盖。

When impurities do show significant structural differences to the API, or where significant differences in physicochemical or PK properties are known and expected to affect the toxicity profile of the impurity, or where significant differences are predicted by computational tools, the impurity should be considered non-API-like and the next step in the flow chart should be followed.

当杂质与原料药在结构上存在显著差异，或理化或PK特性存在显著差异并预计会影响杂质的毒性特征，或通过计算工具预测存在显著差异时，应将该杂质视为非原料药类杂质，并遵循流程图中的下一步。

Furthermore, there are very rare exceptions where a non-significant change of the chemical structure could considerably change the toxicological potency of a molecule (e.g. enantiomers, diastereomers). As in medicinal chemistry, such activity cliffs can be relevant for toxicity, especially where a key event is based on binding of the chemical entity to a specific site (Stumpfe et al., 2019), e.g. the S-enantiomer of thalidomide (Eriksson et al., 2000) exhibits teratogenic activity, while the R-enantiomer acts as a sedative; or the acid form of cholesterol-lowering statins that can condensate to the lactone form. These lactones can be present as impurities or formed endogenously. Despite the high structural similarity, the statin lactones may inhibit Complex III of the mitochondrial respiratory chain more potently and cause myopathy (Schirris et al., 2015). Cases where an API-like NMI with a non-significant change in relation to the API would result in increased toxicity is expected to be very rare. Identification of such potent NMIs should be based on identification of toxicophores or existing knowledge through available literature.

此外，极少数情况下，化学结构的微小变化可能会显著改变分子的毒理强度（例如对映异构体、非对映异构体）。正如在药物化学中，这种活性悬崖可能与毒性相关，尤其是在关键事件基于化学实体与特定位点结合的情况下（Stumpfe et al., 2019），例如沙利度胺的S-对映异构体（Eriksson et al., 2000）具有致畸活性，而R-对映异构体则具有镇静作用；或降胆固醇他汀类药物的酸形式可缩合为内酯形式。这些内酯可能作为杂质存在或在体内形成。尽管结构高度相似，他汀类内酯可能更强烈地抑制线粒体呼吸链复合体III并导致肌病（Schirris et al., 2015）。预期原料药类NMI与原料药相比仅发生微小结构变化却导致毒性增加的情况极为罕见。识别此类高毒性NMI应基于毒理基团的鉴别或通过现有文献知识。

### 5.3.3. Grouping approach

5.3.3. 分组方法

Alternative to the single surrogate approach, a grouping approach can be used, where several similar [impurities](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8 "分类:杂质") are grouped, containing the same toxicophores and functional groups, which allows the detection of trends across endpoints. Again, as defined for the read-across approach, adequate similarity must be demonstrated for chemical-structural, physicochemical, and [PK](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%8D%AF%E4%BB%A3%E5%8A%A8%E5%8A%9B%E5%AD%A6 "分类:药代动力学") properties to group the impurities. Based on the assessment of similarity of the grouped impurities, this may then allow for an overall AL to be derived for the group, which should be based on the most conservatively derived AL unless otherwise justified. Data from the grouping approach may also be used to de-risk the toxicological concern for the impurity, if it can be adequately demonstrated that the group of impurities is not likely to present any safety risk at the specified level of the impurity.

作为单一替代化合物方法的替代方案，可以采用分组方法，将具有相同毒理基团和功能基团的多个相似杂质分组，从而检测各终点之间的趋势。同样，如交叉读取方法所定义，必须证明杂质在化学结构、理化和药代动力学特性方面具有足够的相似性才能进行分组。基于对分组杂质相似性的评估，可以为该组推导出一个总体的AL，该AL应基于最保守推导出的AL，除非有其他充分理由。如果能够充分证明该组杂质在指定杂质水平下不太可能带来任何安全风险，则分组方法的数据也可用于降低杂质的毒理担忧。

## 5.4. Level of concern considerations

5.4. 关注程度的考虑

The safety assessment of a non-mutagenic pharmaceutical impurity is a structured process, as depicted in the flowchart (Figure 1). An essential component of this process is the qualitative evaluation of the "level of concern," which relies on the factors presented in Figure 2. A detailed methodology, including examples, is provided in Annex 2. This tool is not intended to quantify risk, but rather to provide an integrated framework to guide decision-making at key stages.

非致突变性药物杂质的安全评估是一个结构化的过程，如流程图（图1）所示。这一过程的一个基本组成部分是对“关注水平”的定性评估，其依赖于图2中列出的因素。详细的评估方法（包括示例）见附录2。该工具的目的不是量化风险，而是提供一个综合框架，以在关键阶段指导决策。

The level of concern assessment is performed at two distinct points:

关注水平评估在两个不同的阶段进行：

**Step 1 – Initial screening**

**步骤1 - 初步筛选**

When an impurity cannot be qualified through straightforward approaches (e.g. existing guidelines, human metabolite status, structural similarity to the API), a preliminary level of concern assessment is conducted. At this stage, the factors in Figure 2 are used to determine whether further toxicological evaluation is warranted. By considering elements, such as the daily intake of the NMI, duration of treatment, clinical indication, and target population, it may be concluded that the NMI poses a sufficiently low concern to be considered qualified without additional data.

当杂质无法通过简单的方法（例如现有指南、人体代谢物状态、与原料药的结构相似性）进行界定时，需进行初步的关注水平评估。在此阶段，使用图2中的因素来判断是否需要进一步的毒理学评估。通过考虑诸如NMI的每日摄入量、治疗持续时间、临床适应症以及目标人群等因素，可能得出该NMI的关注水平足够低，无需额外数据即可视为已界定的结论。

**Step 2 – Risk Judgement**

**步骤2 - 风险判断**

If further evaluation is needed, specific toxicological data are generated (e.g., determination of an Acceptable Limit (AL) or an in silico analysis). Significantly, if the impurity level is above the calculated AL, a second more comprehensive level of concern assessment should be performed. When computational toxicology is used to assess the impurity, a second level of concern assessment should be considered due to the associated uncertainties of the computational models. The objective of this second-tier assessment is to ensure the potential risk is acceptable within the overall context of the treatment. The acquired toxicological data are weighed against the full set of factors in Figure 2, with special attention given to the impurity's intrinsic properties (e.g., [bioavailability](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%94%9F%E7%89%A9%E5%88%A9%E7%94%A8%E5%BA%A6 "分类:生物利用度"), persistence) and the vulnerabilities of the patient population (e.g., children, patients with renal or hepatic impairment). This integrated analysis will determine whether the risk is acceptable, if the impurity limit must be reduced, or if additional studies are warranted. Ultimately, each risk factor must be evaluated in the context of all others to support a scientifically sound and balanced judgement.

如果需要进一步评估，则生成特定的毒理学数据（例如确定可接受限值（AL）或进行计算机模拟分析）。重要的是，如果杂质水平高于计算得出的AL，则应进行第二次更全面的关注水平评估。当使用计算机毒理学方法评估杂质时，由于计算模型存在相关不确定性，应考虑进行第二次关注水平评估。第二次评估的目的是确保在整体治疗背景下潜在风险是可接受的。将获得的毒理学数据与图2中列出的全部因素进行权衡，特别关注杂质的内在特性（例如生物利用度、持久性）以及患者人群的易感性（例如儿童、肾功能或肝损害患者）。这种综合分析将确定风险是否可接受，是否需要降低杂质限度，或是否需要进一步研究。最终，每个风险因素都必须结合所有其他因素进行评估，以支持科学合理且平衡的判断。

### 5.4.1. Key Factors for Each Step

5.4.1. 各步骤的关键因素

The following section outlines the priority factors from Figure 2 that should be considered during each stage of the level of concern assessment

以下部分列出了图2中在关注水平评估的每个阶段应考虑的优先因素。

_**Key Factors for Step 1 (Triage and Scoping)**_

_**步骤1（初步筛选和范围界定）的关键因素**_

At this stage, the objective is to make an initial decision on the need to proceed with further evaluation. The focus is therefore on the most impactful and well-defined factors of the analysis that do not require in depth evaluation to obtain the information.

在此阶段，目标是就是否需要进一步评估做出初步判断。因此，重点放在分析中影响最大且定义明确的因素上，这些因素无需深入评估即可获得相关信息。

**Daily intake (of [impurity](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8 "分类:杂质")):** This is the primary starting point. A very low exposure (e.g., < TTC) can justify a rapid conclusion of the process.

每日摄入量（杂质）：这是主要的起始点。暴露量非常低（例如，< TTC）可以作为快速结束评估过程的依据。

**Duration of treatment:** A lifetime (chronic) treatment is of significantly higher concern than a short-term or single-dose ("once in a lifetime") treatment.

治疗持续时间：终身（慢性）治疗的关注水平显著高于短期或单次给药（“一生一次”）治疗。

**Clinical indication/severity of the condition:** An impurity in a medicinal product for a life-threatening disease (e.g., chemotherapy) is viewed with greater tolerance than an impurity in a medicinal product for a benign condition.

临床适应症/病情严重程度：用于危及生命疾病（例如化疗）的药品中的杂质比用于良性疾病的药品中的杂质更容易被接受。

_**Key Factors for Step 2 (Evaluation and Final Decision)**_

_**步骤2（评估和最终决定）的关键因素**_

At this stage, more nuanced factors are integrated to refine the contextual understanding of the identified hazard that requires an acquisition of more product-specific information.

在此阶段，整合更多细微的因素，以深化对已识别危害的背景理解，这需要获取更多产品特定的信息。

**Clinical Safety Margin (of DS):** Qualification is supported by the safety margin established during non-clinical testing of the DS. When the exposure levels of impurities in the toxicology batches significantly exceed the intended clinical exposure (large safety margin), and no adverse effects are observed at the NOAEL, the impurities are considered to have a low toxicological concern.

原料药的临床安全范围：非临床试验中建立的安全范围支持杂质的界定。当毒理学批次中杂质的暴露水平显著高于预期临床暴露水平（安全范围大），且在NOAEL下未观察到不良反应时，杂质被认为具有较低的毒理学关注水平。

**Physicochemical and [pharmacokinetic](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%8D%AF%E4%BB%A3%E5%8A%A8%E5%8A%9B%E5%AD%A6 "分类:药代动力学") properties:** These properties could influence systemic exposure and organ-specific toxicity and are essential for interpreting both in vivo and in silico data and therefore crucial for interpreting the toxicological data. A persistent and highly bioavailable impurity is more concerning than one that is rapidly degraded and eliminated, even if their calculated AL was the same.

理化性质和药代动力学特性：这些特性可能影响全身暴露和器官特异性毒性，对于解释体内和计算机模拟数据至关重要，因此对于解释毒理学数据也至关重要。即使计算的AL相同，持久且生物利用度高的杂质比迅速降解和清除的杂质更令人关注。

**[Route of administration](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%BB%99%E8%8D%AF%E9%80%94%E5%BE%84 "分类:给药途径"):** The route of administration (e.g., intrathecal) can significantly increase organ-specific exposure and thus the level of concern, even for a low systemic exposure.

给药途径：给药途径（例如鞘内给药）可能会显著增加器官特异性暴露，从而提高关注水平，即使全身暴露较低也是如此。

**Patient population (specific subpopulations):** A limit deemed safe for general target patients may be inappropriate for vulnerable subpopulations, e.g. pediatric patients, patients with underlying conditions such as renal or hepatic impairment, who may exhibit reduced clearance and increased systemic exposure, pregnant women.

患者人群（特定亚人群）：对一般目标患者群体而言安全的限度可能不适用于易感亚人群，例如儿童患者、基础疾病（如肾功能或肝损害）患者，这些人群可能表现出清除率降低和全身暴露增加，妊娠妇女。

### 5.4.2. Exposure level considerations

5.4.2. 暴露水平的考量

_**Clinical safety margin of the drug substance**_

_**原料药的临床安全范围**_

When impurities are present in DS batches that have been evaluated in non-clinical safety studies, the [NOAEL](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:NOAEL "分类:NOAEL") typically reflects the toxicological profile of the [API](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E5%8E%9F%E6%96%99%E8%8D%AF "分类:原料药"), as impurities are not usually present at levels sufficient to drive toxicity unless specifically investigated. Nevertheless, a large safety margin— defined as the ratio between the exposure at the NOAEL in animal studies and the maximal anticipated clinical exposure—suggests low toxicity. This implies that both the API and any co-present impurities are unlikely to pose significant safety concerns at the tested levels. Thus, this information may support the toxicological qualification of impurities that were present in the test material used during (non-) clinical safety studies, provided their levels were representative of the intended clinical exposure.

当原料药批次中存在杂质且已进行了非临床安全性研究时，NOAEL通常反映原料药的毒理学特征，因为杂质通常不会以足以引发毒性的水平存在，除非专门进行了调查。然而，较大的安全范围 - 定义为动物研究中NOAEL下的暴露量与最大预期临床暴露量之间的比值 - 表明毒性较低。这意味着在测试水平下，原料药及其共存杂质均不太可能带来显著的安全性担忧。因此，如果杂质水平能够代表预期临床暴露情况，则本信息可支持对非临床/临床安全性研究中所用试验物料中含有的杂质进行毒理学评估。

_**Absolute daily intake of impurity**_

_**杂质的绝对每日摄入量**_

The absolute daily dose or exposure to an impurity is a critical factor in evaluating its toxicological risk. As illustrated in Figure 2, a daily dose of the NMI at or below the [TTC](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:TTC "分类:TTC") is generally considered to represent a low concern. This concept will be explained below. In the regulatory context of pharmaceutical impurity assessments, a QT of 1 mg/day (or 0.15% whichever is lower) has been applied for DSs with a MDD of 667 mg/day or higher ([ICH Q3A(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3A_\(R2\)_Impurities_in_new_drug_substances "EMA ICH Q3A (R2) Impurities in new drug substances")). Assuming a body weight of 50 kg, this corresponds to 0.02 mg/kg bw/day.

杂质的绝对每日剂量或暴露量是评估其毒理学风险的关键因素。如图2所示，NMI的每日剂量等于或低于TTC的情况通常被认为属于低关注级别。这一概念将在下文进行解释。在药品杂质评估的监管背景下，对于每日最大剂量（MDD）为667 mg/天或更高的制剂（DSs），已采用1 mg/天的鉴定阈值（QT）（或0.15%，以较低者为准）（ICH Q3A(R2)）。假设人体重为50公斤，这相当于0.02 mg/kg bw/天。

Recent publications (Graham et al., 2021; Harvey et al., 2017; Kenyon et al., 2024; Slikkerveer et al., 2024; Hasselgren et al., 2024) argue that APIs with a NOAEL below 0.02 mg/kg bw/day are extremely rare and that this would support the notion that 1 mg/day should be considered as a generally safe threshold for NMIs. For chemicals of low toxicological concern, exposures below this level of 1 mg/day could indeed be acceptable in most cases. However, this assumption cannot be applied universally. Certain chemical classes and chemotypes – such as organophosphates, carbamates or [beta-lactams](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%CE%92-%E5%86%85%E9%85%B0%E8%83%BA%E7%B1%BB "分类:Β-内酰胺类") – are known to exhibit toxicity at lower exposure levels (Harvey et al., 2017). Therefore, safety concerns cannot be excluded a priori. Additional considerations, such as inter-and intraspecies differences in sensitivity, differences in [bioavailability](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%94%9F%E7%89%A9%E5%88%A9%E7%94%A8%E5%BA%A6 "分类:生物利用度") and duration of exposure – animal studies are often short-term, whereas patients may receive treatments over extensive periods – may affect the risk assessment of an NMI. Although many of these factors have been considered by Kenyon et al. (2024), EMA does not regard the 1 mg/day threshold as a definitive cut-off value. Instead, it is treated as a reference point-the midpoint on the daily dose scale illustrated in Figure 2. Thus, when impurity exposure levels exceed the TTC but remain below 1 mg/day, the level of concern will have to be evaluated, taking into account all relevant factors outlined in Figure 2. If the daily exposure to the NMI exceeds 1 mg/day, the level of concern increases proportionally, warranting more detailed toxicological assessments.

最近的出版物（Graham 等，2021；Harvey 等，2017；Kenyon 等，2024；Slikkerveer 等，2024；Hasselgren 等，2024）认为，NOAEL 低于 0.02 mg/kg bw/day 的原料药极为罕见，这支持了 1 mg/day 应被视为 NMIs 的普遍安全阈值的观点。对于毒理学关注程度较低的化学物质，在大多数情况下，每日暴露量低于该水平（1 mg/天）确实是可接受的。然而，这一假设并不能普遍适用。某些化学类别和化学类型 - 例如有机磷化合物、氨基甲酸酯类或β-内酰胺类 - 在较低暴露水平下即表现出毒性（Harvey 等，2017）。因此，不能预先排除安全性问题。其他考虑因素，例如敏感度的种间和种内差异、生物利用度差异以及暴露持续时间差异 - 动物研究通常是短期的，而患者可能会接受长期治疗 - 可能影响NMI的风险评估。尽管Kenyon等人（2024）已考虑了上述诸多因素，但EMA并不将每日1毫克的阈值视为一个明确的临界值。相反，该数值被视为一个参考点 - 即图2所示每日剂量刻度的中点。因此，当杂质暴露水平超过TTC但仍低于1 mg/天时，需要结合图2中列出的所有相关因素来评估其关注程度。如果每日接触NMI的剂量超过1毫克/天，关注程度将成比例增加，需进行更详细的毒理学评估。

_**Threshold of Toxicological Concern**_

_**毒理学关注阈值**_

The TTC is a risk assessment tool used to evaluate low-level exposure to chemicals with limited toxicological data. As described in [ICH M7(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_M7_R2_Guideline_on_assessment_and_control_of_DNA_reactive_mutagenic_impurities_in_pharmaceuticals_to_limit_potential_carcinogenic_risk_20230718 "EMA ICH M7 R2 Guideline on assessment and control of DNA reactive mutagenic impurities in pharmaceuticals to limit potential carcinogenic risk 20230718"), the TTC is well-established for mutagenic impurities. For non-mutagenic endpoints, the same principle can be applied when insufficient toxicological data are available and read-across is not feasible (see section 5.3.). Since TTC values are endpoint-and route-specific, their applicability must be verified for each case. If the exposure level is below the relevant TTC, no further action is generally required. However, TTC values represent thresholds below which no appreciable risk is expected for most—but not all—chemicals. Therefore, even when exposure is below the TTC, the level of concern must be evaluated in a broader context (see Figure 2).

TTC 是一种风险评估工具，用于评估毒理学数据有限的化学品在低水平暴露情况下的风险。如ICH M7(R2)中所述，TTC已广泛应用于致突变杂质的评估。对于非致突变性终点，在毒理学数据不足且无法进行交叉读取时，也可以应用相同的原则（参见第5.3节）。由于TTC值具有终点特异性和给药途径特异性，因此必须针对每种情况验证其适用性。如果暴露水平低于相关TTC，则通常无需采取进一步措施。然而，TTC值代表了一个阈值，在该阈值以下，大多数（但不是全部）化学品不会产生可察觉的风险。因此，即使暴露量低于TTC，也必须在更广泛的背景下评估关注水平（见图2）。

The international regulatory community has established specific systemic and local toxicity thresholds for extractables and leachables in oral and parenteral formulations. These values are based on a large dataset establishing [PDEs](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:PDE "分类:PDE") for impurities of different potencies, resulting in robust thresholds divided across different durations of administration and these values may inform decision making in evaluating the safety risk of an impurity. Thus, if NMIs in oral or parental medicinal products are below the systemic or local toxicity thresholds in Table 2, they are in general considered to be of low concern and no further data is considered necessary to qualify the impurities. Assessment of higher potency impurities should be based on a review of available impurity-specific data or computational [toxicology](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%AF%92%E7%90%86%E5%AD%A6 "分类:毒理学") alerts based on the presence of toxicophores in the NMI.

国际监管机构已为口服和肠胃外制剂中的溶出物和析出物建立了特定的全身和局部毒性阈值。这些值基于大量数据集建立，用于不同活性杂质的PDE，从而得出根据不同给药持续时间划分的稳健阈值，这些值可用于评估杂质安全性风险的决策。因此，如果口服或肠胃外药品中的NMI低于表2中的全身或局部毒性阈值，则通常认为其关注程度较低，无需进一步数据来确认杂质的安全性。高活性杂质的评估应基于可获得的杂质特异性数据的审查，或基于NMI中存在毒理基团的计算毒理学预警。

Toxicity threshold values for inhalation and dermal/transdermal routes have been established based upon parenteral thresholds in lieu of available PDE values. These thresholds are currently being discussed in the international regulatory community for extractables and leachables and are considered relevant for other non-mutagenic impurities.

吸入和皮肤/透皮途径的毒性阈值是基于肠胃外阈值建立的，以替代可用的PDE值。这些阈值目前正在国际监管机构中讨论，适用于溶出物和析出物，并被认为适用于其他非致突变性杂质。

Within the food safety domain, [TTC](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:TTC "分类:TTC") values have been established for various chemical classes. For organophosphates and carbamates, the relevant TTC is 0.3 µg/kg bw/day. All other chemicals are grouped according to the Cramer classification, with TTC values of: Cramer Class I: 30 µg/kg bw/day; Cramer Class II: 9 µg/kg bw/day and Cramer Class III: 1.5 µg/kg bw/day (EFSA, 2019; Munro et al., 1996). These values are applicable to NMIs in orally administered pharmaceuticals, provided that the impurity is not exempted from the application of the TTC (e.g. steroids, bio-accumulative substances, or those with known high potency). Alternative TTC values derived using refined methodologies (e.g. Tluczkiewicz et al., 2011) are generally consistent with the above. Future refinements of the classification of chemicals may emerge from initiatives such as the Food and Drug Administration's Expanded Decision Tree (EDT) Project, which aims to modernise the Cramer classification and improve TTC applicability (Stice and Adams, 2025).

在食品安全领域，已为各类化学品建立了TTC值。对于有机磷和氨基甲酸酯类化合物，相关的TTC为0.3 µg/kg bw/天。其他所有化学品根据Cramer分类进行归类，其TTC值为：Cramer I类：30 µg/kg bw/天；Cramer II类：9 µg/kg bw/天；Cramer III类：1.5 µg/kg bw/天（EFSA，2019；Munro等，1996）。这些值适用于口服给药药品中的NMI，前提是该杂质未被豁免TTC的应用（例如类固醇、生物蓄积性物质或已知高活性物质）。使用改进方法推导出的替代TTC值（例如Tluczkiewicz等，2011）通常与上述值一致。未来化学品分类的改进可能来自诸如美国食品药品管理局扩展决策树（EDT）项目等倡议，该项目旨在现代化Cramer分类并改进TTC的适用性（Stice和Adams，2025）。

More work has been done to derive TTC values for other routes of exposure such as inhalation (e.g. Escher et al., 2010; Tluczkiewicz et al., 2016; Nelms and Patlewicz, 2020). Cramer classification appeared to be a less suitable approach for this route. For the chemicals that were grouped as toxic or reactive, TTC values in the range of 2-4 µg/day have been calculated. This compares reasonably well with the QT of 5 µg/day for leachables in orally-inhaled, and nasal DPs as derived by the PQRI consortium (Ball et al., 2007). The latter mentions that irritating chemicals, including aldehydes, nitriles and isocyanates, as well as metals and metal salts, could be of concern below this value. A TTC of 4 µg/day can be considered as a practical value for impurities in medicinal products administered via inhalation, provided they do not belong to one of the chemical classes of high concern.

对于其他暴露途径（如吸入）的TTC值也进行了更多研究（例如Escher等，2010；Tluczkiewicz等，2016；Nelms和Patlewicz，2020）。Cramer分类对于该途径似乎不太适用。对于被归类为有毒或反应性强的化学品，计算出的TTC值范围为2-4 µg/天。这与PQRI联盟推导的经口吸入和鼻用DP中析出物的QT值5 µg/天相比是合理的（Ball等，2007）。后者指出，刺激性化学品，包括醛类、腈类和异氰酸酯类，以及金属和金属盐，在低于该值时也可能值得关注。对于通过吸入途径给药的药品中的杂质，只要不属于高度关注的化学类别，可将4 µg/天作为实用的TTC值。

For the dermal [route of administration](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%BB%99%E8%8D%AF%E9%80%94%E5%BE%84 "分类:给药途径"), skin sensitisation is considered the most sensitive non-mutagenic endpoint when it concerns reactive chemicals, including high potency category (HPC) chemicals (Roberts et al_._, 2015; Nishijo et al_._, 2020). Based on an extensive review of literature data, dermal sensitisation thresholds (DST) have been calculated for non-reactive, reactive and HPC chemicals, which are 900, 64, and 1.5 μg/cm2, respectively (Safford et al. 2008, Safford et al. 2015; Nishijo et al. 2020, Parris et al., 2024). These were modified later to 710, 73, and 1.0 μg/cm2, respectively (Chilton et al., 2022). These values could be used for dermal products, to address the concern for dermal sensitisation. For other non-mutagenic endpoints, systemic exposure needs to be considered, taking into account the surface to which the medicinal product is applied and the degree of dermal absorption (see below for discussion on [PK](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%8D%AF%E4%BB%A3%E5%8A%A8%E5%8A%9B%E5%AD%A6 "分类:药代动力学") properties and [bioavailability](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%94%9F%E7%89%A9%E5%88%A9%E7%94%A8%E5%BA%A6 "分类:生物利用度")). Once the systemic exposure has been estimated, a comparison with parenteral TTC values can be made.

对于经皮给药途径，当涉及反应性化学品，包括高活性类别（HPC）化学品时，皮肤致敏被认为是比其他非基因毒性终点更为敏感的终点（Roberts等，2015；Nishijo等，2020）。基于对文献数据的广泛回顾，已计算出非反应性、反应性和HPC化学品的经皮致敏阈值（DST），分别为900、64和1.5 μg/cm²（Safford等，2008；Safford等，2015；Nishijo等，2020；Parris等，2024）。这些数值随后被修正为710、73和1.0 μg/cm²（Chilton等，2022）。这些数值可用于经皮产品，以评估经皮致敏的风险。对于其他非基因毒性终点，需要考虑系统暴露量，同时考虑药品所应用的表面积和经皮吸收程度（见下文关于药代动力学特性和生物利用度的讨论）。一旦估算出系统暴露量，就可以与注射途径的TTC值进行比较。

Intramuscular, subcutaneous, and intravenous routes of administration are the main parenteral routes for pharmaceuticals. Where parenteral is discussed here, any of these three routes are considered. For other parenteral routes, specific considerations may apply as the pharmaceutical may be administered into a small compartment or in close contact with sensitive tissue (e.g. ophthalmic products or intrathecally, epidural, or sub-arachnoidally administered products). For any route of administration not discussed in this reflection paper, like intravitreal route of administration, a case-by-case discussion would be needed. In the literature, reports have been published describing various approaches to derive parenteral (systemic, internal) TTC values. Arnot et al. (2022) used the Munro database to derive internal NOELs (mg/kg/day) by combining the oral [NOEL](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:NOEL "分类:NOEL") values with available PK data, where such data were not available, by applying Physiologically Based Pharmacokinetic (PBPK) modelling to estimate internal NOELs. Internal TTC (iTTC) values were derived using the fifth percentile as cut-off and dividing this by 100. For whole body, an iTTC value of 0.5 nmol/kg was proposed. Taking a human body weight of 50 kg and using the median molecular weight (220) of the compounds in the Munro dataset, this can be transformed to 5.5 µg/day. This value is remarkably similar to the one that was derived by Partosch et al. (2015), who used different databases and arrived at an iTTC for Cramer class II/III compounds of 0.1 µg/kg bw/day or 5 µg/day for a person of 50 kg. In [ICH Q3D](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3D_Elemental_impurities_Adopted_20190328 "EMA ICH Q3D Elemental impurities Adopted 20190328") modifying factors have been proposed allowing the derivation of a parenteral PDE from an oral PDE (mg/day) taking into consideration the oral bioavailability. In worst cases, where oral bioavailability data are not available, a parenteral PDE can be extrapolated by dividing the oral PDE with a modifying factor of 100. For most of the [elemental impurities](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E5%85%83%E7%B4%A0%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8 "分类:元素杂质"), parenteral PDEs have been determined by dividing the oral [PDE](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:PDE "分类:PDE") by a factor of 10, which assumes that estimating oral bioavailability at 10% is sufficiently conservative. If the modifying factor of 10 were to be applied to the Cramer class TTCs, we would arrive at systemic [TTC](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:TTC "分类:TTC") values of 150, 45 and 7.5 µg/day for a 50 kg person for Cramer class I, II, and III compounds, respectively. In this approach, the systemic TTC for class III compounds is quite close to the iTTC values proposed by Arnot et al. (2022) and Partosch et al. (2015). These values differ, however, from the lifetime parenteral TTC value of 35 µg/day that took into consideration the 422 compounds in the Extractables and Leachables Safety Information Exchange (ELSIE) database. This database contains toxicity data for reported or presumed extractables and leachables (Masuda-Herrera et al_._, 2022). In this publication, corrections for bioavailability were based either on actual PK data or on an in silico tool for estimating bioavailability. If no NOAEL was available, the [LOAEL](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:LOAEL "分类:LOAEL") was chosen, and an additional correction factor was used. The estimated systemic values were divided by 100.

肌肉注射、皮下注射和静脉注射是药品的主要非经口给药途径。此处讨论的“非经口途径”包括这三种途径中的任何一种。对于其他非经口给药途径，可能需要特别考虑，因为药品可能被注射到一个小的腔室中或与敏感组织密切接触（例如眼科产品或鞘内、硬膜外或蛛网膜下腔给药的产品）。对于本思考性文件中未讨论的任何其他给药途径，如玻璃体内给药途径，需要进行个案讨论。在文献中已有报告描述了多种推导非经口（系统性、内部）TTC值的方法。Arnot等（2022）使用Munro数据库，通过将口服NOEL值与可用的药代动力学数据结合来推导内部NOEL（mg/kg/天）；在缺乏此类数据的情况下，应用基于生理的药代动力学（PBPK）模型来估算内部NOEL。内部TTC（iTTC）值是通过使用第五百分位数作为临界值并将其除以100得出的。对于全身，提出了0.5 nmol/kg的iTTC值。假设人体重为50 kg，并使用Munro数据集中化合物的中位分子量（220），可将其转换为5.5 µg/天。该数值与Partosch等（2015）推导出的数值非常相似，后者使用了不同的数据库，并得出针对Cramer II/III类化合物的iTTC为0.1 µg/kg bw/天，或对于50 kg的人为5 µg/天。在ICH Q3D中提出了修改因子，允许根据口服PDE（mg/天）推导出非经口PDE，同时考虑口服生物利用度。在最不利的情况下，如果缺乏口服生物利用度数据，可以通过将口服PDE除以100的修改因子来外推非经口PDE。对于大多数元素杂质，非经口PDE是通过将口服PDE除以10的因子确定的，这假设口服生物利用度为10%是足够保守的。如果将10的修改因子应用于Cramer类别的TTC，则对于50 kg的人，Cramer I、II和III类化合物的系统性TTC值分别为150、45和7.5 µg/天。在这种方法中，III类化合物的系统性TTC值与Arnot等（2022）和Partosch等（2015）提出的iTTC值非常接近。然而，这些数值与基于Extractables and Leachables Safety Information Exchange（ELSIE）数据库中422种化合物得出的35 µg/天的终生非经口TTC值不同。该数据库包含报告或推测的溶出物和析出物的毒性数据（Masuda-Herrera等，2022）。在该出版物中，生物利用度的校正依据是实际的药代动力学数据或用于估算生物利用度的in silico工具。如果没有NOAEL数据，则选择LOAEL，并使用额外的校正因子。估算的系统性数值被除以100。

In addition to the threshold values described above, the route-dependent differences in toxicity must be specifically considered. These route-specific sensitivities are also reflected in the different TTC values; a principle now discussed in relation to extractables and leachables, which proposes distinct systemic and local toxicity thresholds based on the [route of administration](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%BB%99%E8%8D%AF%E9%80%94%E5%BE%84 "分类:给药途径"). For instance, orally inhaled and nasal DPs are delivered to the respiratory tract where tissues are receptive to sensitisation and irritation, a risk acknowledged in guidelines such as ICH S4, which covers inhalation toxicity studies. Thus, these endpoints are often the most critical for this route.

除了上述阈值外，还必须特别考虑毒性作用的途径相关差异。这些途径特异性敏感性也反映在不同的TTC值中；这一原则目前在溶出物和析出物的背景下被讨论，提出基于给药途径的不同系统性和局部毒性阈值。例如，口服吸入和鼻腔给药的产品被递送至呼吸道，该部位的组织对致敏和刺激敏感，这一风险在ICH S4等指南中已被认可，该指南涵盖吸入毒性研究。因此，这些终点通常是该途径最关键的终点。

When dermally applied, sensitisation is the most sensitive endpoint when HPC chemicals are concerned, and possibly also when the impurity is a non-HPC reactive chemical. The concept of dermal sensitisation threshold (DST) is a key tool for this assessment. However, given the possibility of dermal absorption, for the dermal route systemic toxicity should be considered as well. For some specific routes of administration into small confined spaces such as intrathecal, epidural or sub-arachnoidal, the relatively high local concentration is an additional risk factor. In these situations, an estimate of the local concentration would be a better parameter for evaluation than the daily dose. In addition, the high sensitivity of central nervous system (CNS) tissues needs to be considered for these special routes.

当高活性化学品经皮应用时，致敏是最敏感的终点，当杂质为非高活性反应性化学品时也可能如此。经皮致敏阈值（DST）的概念是此类评估的关键工具。然而，鉴于经皮吸收的可能性，对于经皮给药途径还应考虑系统毒性。对于一些特定的给药途径，如鞘内、硬膜外或蛛网膜下腔等小空间内的给药，相对较高的局部浓度是一个额外的风险因素。在这种情况下，局部浓度的估算值比每日剂量更适合用于评估。此外，对于这些特殊给药途径，还需考虑中枢神经系统（CNS）组织的高敏感性。

_**[杂质的理化和药代动力学性质 / 生物利用度](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8 "分类:杂质")**_

By definition, medicinal products administered via intravenous injection have 100% bioavailability. Due to little or no metabolism in the skin or muscle, most medicinal products show between 60 and 100% bioavailability after subcutaneous and intramuscular injections (Stielow et al., 2023). Thus, parenteral routes pose the highest concern, as opposed to medicinal products administered via routes where limited absorption may reduce the systemic exposure. This is a foundational [PK](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%8D%AF%E4%BB%A3%E5%8A%A8%E5%8A%9B%E5%AD%A6 "分类:药代动力学") principle applied across all medicinal product development. Clearly, this is only relevant with respect to systemic toxicity. As discussed above, local toxicity is to be considered separately.

根据定义，通过静脉注射给药的药品具有100%的生物利用度。由于皮肤或肌肉中的代谢极少，大多数药品在皮下和肌内注射后显示出60%至100%的生物利用度（Stielow等人，2023年）。因此，非经肠道给药途径带来的风险最高，与那些吸收有限可能降低全身暴露的给药途径相比。这是所有药品开发中应用的基础药代动力学原则。显然，这仅与全身毒性相关。如上所述，局部毒性应单独考虑。

Information on absorption and bioavailability of the impurity may be retrieved from the literature. In the absence of such data, physicochemical (PC) properties can be considered to estimate bioavailability. These properties are also used in in silico tools to estimate bioavailability. The use of such predictive tools is strongly endorsed by modern regulatory approaches, most notably in [ICH M7(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_M7_R2_Guideline_on_assessment_and_control_of_DNA_reactive_mutagenic_impurities_in_pharmaceuticals_to_limit_potential_carcinogenic_risk_20230718 "EMA ICH M7 R2 Guideline on assessment and control of DNA reactive mutagenic impurities in pharmaceuticals to limit potential carcinogenic risk 20230718") for mutagenicity assessment. As these tools have their limitations, predictability can be improved by supporting experimental NAM data such as transport across Caco-2 cells and metabolism in hepatic models, a strategy encouraged by agencies like the FDA in its Predictive Toxicology Roadmap. In case the impurity exists as (dia)stereoisomers, potential effects on PK properties need to be considered. In the absence of factual data to the contrary, bioavailability of impurities in medicinal products administered via the respiratory route is considered to be (close to) 100%.

关于杂质吸收和生物利用度的信息可从文献中获取。在缺乏此类数据的情况下，可以考虑理化（PC）性质以估算生物利用度。这些性质也用于计算机模拟工具中以估算生物利用度。现代监管方法强烈支持使用此类预测工具，尤其是在ICH M7(R2)中用于致突变性评估。由于这些工具存在局限性，可通过支持性的实验NAM数据（如Caco-2细胞跨膜转运和肝模型中的代谢）来提高预测能力，这一策略得到了FDA在其预测毒理路线图中等机构的鼓励。如果杂质以（消旋）立体异构体形式存在，则需要考虑其对药代动力学性质的潜在影响。在缺乏实际数据的情况下，认为通过呼吸道给药的药品中的杂质生物利用度为（接近）100%。

Impurities that are poorly degraded or eliminated otherwise increase the level of concern as such impurities can accumulate and, even with low daily exposures, may reach tissue concentrations where [adverse effects](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E4%B8%8D%E8%89%AF%E5%8F%8D%E5%BA%94 "分类:不良反应") could occur. Examples are highly bio accumulative impurities, where even short-term dosing can result in long term exposure, as is the case for example for certain per-and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS). This potential for accumulation is a key reason for conducting the [repeated-dose toxicity](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E9%87%8D%E5%A4%8D%E7%BB%99%E8%8D%AF%E6%AF%92%E6%80%A7 "分类:重复给药毒性") studies outlined in [ICH M3(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_M3_\(R2\)_Non-clinical_safety_studies_for_the_conduct_of_human_clinical_trials_for_pharmaceuticals "EMA ICH M3 (R2) Non-clinical safety studies for the conduct of human clinical trials for pharmaceuticals").

难以降解或难以消除的杂质会增加关注程度，因为此类杂质可能积累，并且即使每日暴露量较低，也可能达到可能产生不良效应的组织浓度。例如某些高度生物蓄积性的杂质，即使短期给药也可能导致长期暴露，例如某些全氟和多氟烷基物质（PFAS）的情况。这种蓄积潜力是开展ICH M3(R2)中所述重复给药毒性研究的主要原因之一。

### 5.4.3. Clinical considerations

5.4.3. 临床考虑

In the level of concern analysis, a case-by-case approach that considers the specifics of the target population and therapeutic indication is essential to define appropriate [specification](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%B4%A8%E9%87%8F%E6%A0%87%E5%87%86 "分类:质量标准") limits for [impurities](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8 "分类:杂质"). This should also take into consideration the duration of treatment.

在关注程度分析中，应采用个案处理的方法，考虑目标人群和治疗适应症的具体情况，以定义杂质的适当质量标准限度。这也应考虑治疗持续时间。

_**Duration of treatment**_

_**治疗持续时间**_

The treatment duration is a key factor to consider when determining the level of concern. For short-term treatments, the level of concern is usually lower, and the principle of applying higher QTs for shorter exposure durations is well-established and consistent with approaches in several ICH impurity guidelines. For example, the core guidelines for non-mutagenic organic impurities, [ICH Q3A(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3A_\(R2\)_Impurities_in_new_drug_substances "EMA ICH Q3A (R2) Impurities in new drug substances") and [ICH Q3B(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3B_\(R2\)_Impurities_in_new_drug_products "EMA ICH Q3B (R2) Impurities in new drug products"), set QTs primarily designed for lifetime exposure. Furthermore, other guidelines like I[CH Q3C(R9)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3C_R9_Guideline_on_impurities_guideline_for_residual_solvents_20240405 "EMA ICH Q3C R9 Guideline on impurities guideline for residual solvents 20240405") (for [residual solvents](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%AE%8B%E7%95%99%E6%BA%B6%E5%89%82 "分类:残留溶剂")) and [ICH Q3D](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3D_Elemental_impurities_Adopted_20190328 "EMA ICH Q3D Elemental impurities Adopted 20190328") (for [elemental impurities](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E5%85%83%E7%B4%A0%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8 "分类:元素杂质")) both indicate that it may be acceptable to exceed their recommended limits for short-term dosing (i.e., ≤ 30 days). Recommended threshold values in Table 2 take administration of shorter duration into account. Conversely, chronic treatments, particularly those that last throughout a patient's lifetime, necessitate a more thorough evaluation of impurity levels due to increased cumulative exposure. Therefore, when treatment duration increases, also the level of concern will increase.

治疗持续时间是确定关注程度时需要考虑的关键因素。对于短期治疗，关注程度通常较低，对较短暴露时间应用较高限度的原则已被广泛接受，并与多个ICH杂质指南中的方法一致。例如，非致突变性有机杂质的核心指南ICH Q3A(R2)和ICH Q3B(R2)设定的限度主要是针对终身暴露设计的。此外，其他指南如ICH Q3C(R9)（关于残留溶剂）和ICH Q3D（关于元素杂质）均指出，在短期给药（即≤30天）的情况下，超过其推荐限度可能是可以接受的。表2中推荐的阈值考虑了较短时间的给药情况。相反，对于持续整个患者生命周期的慢性治疗，由于累积暴露增加，需要对杂质水平进行更全面的评估。因此，当治疗持续时间增加时，关注程度也会增加。

_**Clinical indication**_

_**临床适应症**_

The clinical indication should be considered as a critical factor, as stipulated in the ICH Q3A and Q3B guidelines. These guidelines provide the primary criteria for qualifying impurities but explicitly permit changes to the QTs based on a scientific rationale that includes the clinical context.

应将临床适应症视为一个关键因素，如ICH Q3A和Q3B指南中所规定的。这些指南提供了杂质界定的主要标准，但明确允许基于包括临床背景在内的科学依据对限度进行调整。

In the context of severe or life-threatening diseases, the presence of impurities may be justified due to a different benefit-risk balance. This is most clearly articulated in the [ICH S9](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_S9_Non-clinical_evaluation_for_anticancer_pharmaceuticals "EMA ICH S9 Non-clinical evaluation for anticancer pharmaceuticals") guideline, which specifically addresses the management of impurities in anti-cancer medications, noting that applying the same controls as for less severe conditions is inappropriate. This principle is a central component of the benefit-risk assessment frameworks used by major regulatory authorities. Furthermore, alterations in the clinical applications of marketed products may necessitate the re-assessment of existing impurity [specifications](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%B4%A8%E9%87%8F%E6%A0%87%E5%87%86 "分类:质量标准").

在严重或危及生命的疾病情况下，由于不同的效益-风险平衡，可能存在杂质的合理性。这一点在ICH S9指南中表述得最为明确，该指南专门针对抗癌药物中杂质的管理，指出对较不严重疾病所采用的相同控制措施并不合适。这一原则是主要监管机构所使用的效益-风险评估框架的核心组成部分。此外，已上市产品临床应用的改变可能需要重新评估现有的杂质质量标准。

_**Target population**_

_**目标人群**_

The target populations should be considered in establishing the level of concern. The sensitivity to toxic effects can vary considerably among different groups. Key points to consider are:

在确定关注程度时应考虑目标人群。不同人群组对毒性效应的敏感性可能有很大差异。需要考虑的关键点包括：

- Paediatric populations: Children are often more susceptible to the toxic effects of impurities due to developmental differences in [ADME](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:ADME "分类:ADME") (Absorption, Distribution, Metabolism, and Excretion). The specific needs and vulnerabilities of this population are detailed in [ICH E11(R1)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_E11_Clinical_investigation_of_medicinal_products_in_the_paediatric_population "EMA ICH E11 Clinical investigation of medicinal products in the paediatric population"), Clinical Investigation of Medicinal Products in the Paediatric Population.
    
    儿科人群：由于在吸收、分布、代谢和排泄（ADME）方面的发育差异，儿童往往对杂质的毒性作用更为敏感。ICH E11(R1)《儿科人群中药品的临床研究》详细说明了这一人群的具体需求和易感性。
    

- Patients with renal or hepatic disease: Impaired elimination can lead to increased concentrations of impurities. EMA have published specific guidance documents requiring [PK](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%8D%AF%E4%BB%A3%E5%8A%A8%E5%8A%9B%E5%AD%A6 "分类:药代动力学") assessments in these populations to ensure safety.
    
    患有肾或肝疾病患者：消除能力受损可能导致杂质浓度升高。欧洲药品管理局（EMA）已发布专门的指南文件，要求在这些人群中进行药代动力学评估以确保安全性。
    

- Pregnant individuals: Exposure to impurities during pregnancy is concerning due to potential developmental toxicity. The nonclinical assessment of such risks is a core topic of the [ICH S5(R3)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_S5_R3_guideline_on_reproductive_toxicology_Detection_of_toxicity_to_reproduction_for_human_pharmaceuticals_20200217 "EMA ICH S5 R3 guideline on reproductive toxicology Detection of toxicity to reproduction for human pharmaceuticals 20200217") guideline on reproductive toxicology.
    
    妊娠个体：妊娠期间暴露于杂质令人担忧，因为可能存在发育毒性。此类风险的非临床评估是ICH S5(R3)关于生殖毒性的指南的核心主题。
    

## 5.5. Acceptable Level calculation

5.5. 可接受水平的计算

Impurity-specific data and data on a surrogate considered acceptable for applying read-across to the impurity can be used for deriving ALs for an NMI. Impurity or surrogate specific toxicological data should be of high quality in vitro and/or in vivo studies. Deriving an estimate of an AL of exposure for a new NMI in patients by using toxicological data is generally achieved by choosing an endpoint specific Benchmark Response (BMR) in an acceptable study and calculating the Benchmark Dose Lower boundary (BMDL) as a point of departure (PoD). Alternatively, an experimentally defined [NOAEL](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:NOAEL "分类:NOAEL") may be used as a PoD, but generally provides a less accurate PoD. The PoD is the anchor point for the AL calculation and applying AFs to correct for variability, uncertainties and known differences between the animal model used and the patient for whom a predicted safe level is needed. Guidance and recommendation for identifying relevant BMR can be found in e.g. WHO EHC 240: Principles for Risk Assessment of Chemicals in Food; Chapter 5 Dose-response assessment and derivation of health-based guidance values (second edition, 2020) or EPA Benchmark Dose Technical Guidance (EPA/100/R-12/001 – June 2012). This reflection paper proposes the AL method, by which similar toxicological principles are used, e.g. as described in [ICH Q3C](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3C_R9_Guideline_on_impurities_guideline_for_residual_solvents_20240405 "EMA ICH Q3C R9 Guideline on impurities guideline for residual solvents 20240405") and [Q3D](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3D_Elemental_impurities_Adopted_20190328 "EMA ICH Q3D Elemental impurities Adopted 20190328"). The AL is considered a conservative estimate of the dose level below which patient safety is not affected and represents the maximal daily dose of an impurity in a pharmaceutical product to which a patient can be exposed during treatment without compromising the patient's health. Although the methodology is similar to the methods for deriving a PDE, the [PDE](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:PDE "分类:PDE") is a generally applicable safe level of exposure for a specific impurity in any product, whereas the AL is a level for an impurity that has been set for a specific product and is considered acceptable in that context. Therefore, specific considerations regarding the level of concern relevant to the product can also be considered (see below). The methodology starts with the selection of the PoD, which can be a BMDL, a NOAEL, or, in the absence of these, a Lowest Observed Adverse Effect Level (LOAEL) or any other acceptable PoD. Subsequently, AFs are applied for inter-and intraspecies variability (AF1 and AF2, respectively), duration of the study from which the PoD is taken (AF3), severity of the toxicity (AF4) and the absence of a BMDL or NOAEL (AF5). Furthermore, a body weight that is appropriate and conservative for the target population (e.g. pediatric patients) should be selected when calculating an AL.

可用于推导NMI的可接受水平（AL）的数据包括杂质特异性数据以及可用于进行交叉读取的替代杂质数据。杂质或替代杂质的毒理学数据应为高质量的体外和/或体内研究。通过使用毒理学数据推导患者中新NMI暴露的AL估计值，通常是通过在可接受的研究中选择特定终点的基准反应（BMR），并计算基准剂量下限（BMDL）作为出发点（PoD）。或者，也可以使用实验确定的无观察不良效应水平（NOAEL）作为出发点，但通常提供的出发点准确性较低。出发点是AL计算的锚定点，并应用修正因子（AFs）来校正变异、不确定性以及动物模型与需要预测安全水平的患者之间的已知差异。有关识别相关BMR的指导和建议，可参见例如WHO EHC 240：《食品化学品风险评估原则》；第5章 剂量-反应评估及基于健康的指导值的推导（第二版，2020年）或美国环保署（EPA）基准剂量技术指南（EPA/100/R-12/001 – 2012年6月）。本思考性文件提出了AL方法，该方法使用类似的毒理学原理，例如在ICH Q3C和Q3D中所述。AL被认为是低于该剂量水平时患者安全性不受影响的保守估计值，并代表患者在治疗期间可暴露于该杂质的最大每日剂量而不影响其健康。尽管该方法与推导PDE的方法类似，但PDE是适用于任何产品中特定杂质的一般安全暴露水平，而AL是针对特定产品中杂质设定的水平，并被认为在该背景下是可以接受的。因此，在确定相关关注水平时，也可以考虑与产品相关的一些特定因素（见下文）。该方法首先选择PoD，可以是BMDL、NOAEL，或在缺乏这些数据时选择最低观察到不良效应水平（LOAEL）或其他可接受的PoD。随后，应用不确定因子（AFs）以考虑种间和种内变异（分别为AF1和AF2）、从PoD所取研究的持续时间（AF3）、毒性的严重程度（AF4）以及缺乏BMDL或NOAEL（AF5）。此外，在计算AL时，应为目标人群（例如儿科患者）选择适当且保守的体重。

Impurities cover a wide chemical space and thus, bioavailability via different administration routes may vary greatly. Often toxicity data are only available for a single route, mostly the oral route, whereas pharmaceuticals are administered via other routes, e.g. a parenteral route. It is therefore recommended that when route-to-route extrapolation is needed, an additional AF is used to account for differences in [bioavailability](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%94%9F%E7%89%A9%E5%88%A9%E7%94%A8%E5%BA%A6 "分类:生物利用度") (AF6).

杂质涵盖广泛的化学空间，因此，通过不同给药途径的生物利用度可能差异很大。通常毒性数据仅适用于单一途径，主要是口服途径，而药品可能通过其他途径给药，例如肠胃外途径。因此，当需要进行途径间外推时，建议使用额外的不确定因子（AF6）以考虑生物利用度的差异。

In this reflection paper, the use of read-across is described as an alternative when insufficient impurity-specific toxicity data are available. To account for the additional uncertainty that may be introduced by relying on the toxicity data of a surrogate, the use of another AF is recommended (AF7).

在本思考性文件中，当杂质特异性毒性数据不足时，描述了使用交叉读取作为替代方法。为了考虑依赖替代杂质毒性数据可能引入的额外不确定性，建议使用另一个不确定因子（AF7）。

The AL can be calculated with the formula:

AL可通过以下公式计算：

_AL_(μ_g_/_d_) = [PoD(_mg/kg/d_) × 50 kg × 1000] / (AF1 × AF2 × AF3 × AF4 × AF5 × AF6 × AF7)

AL（μg/d）= [PoD（mg/kg/d） × 50 kg × 1000] / (AF1 × AF2 × AF3 × AF4 × AF5 × AF6 × AF7)

The use of AFs is described in more detail in the Annex.

AF的使用在附录中有更详细的描述。

The most relevant study should be used to select the PoD, taking into consideration the duration of human exposure, the duration of the animal study, the species used, the route of exposure, the toxicological endpoints monitored, and the quality of the study data.

选择PoD时应使用最相关的研究，同时考虑人体暴露时间、动物研究持续时间、所用物种、暴露途径、监测的毒理学终点以及研究数据的质量。

It may happen that an effect is observed that is not relevant for humans. In that case it could be inappropriate to choose this endpoint as the basis for the PoD and another one should be considered, rather than establish an AL on the basis of this endpoint not considered relevant for humans.

有时可能观察到某种效应，但该效应对人类并不相关。在这种情况下，将该终点作为PoD的基础可能是不合适的，应考虑其他终点，而不是基于该对人类不相关的终点建立AL。

If it is unclear what is the most appropriate PoD, it is acceptable to calculate multiple AL values and select the most conservative value. It is not recommended to utilise LD50 values in AL calculations.

如果不清楚哪个PoD最为合适，可以接受计算多个AL值并选择最保守的值。不建议在AL计算中使用LD50值。

BMDL can be used as PoD. A BMDL makes use of all data on the [dose-response](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E5%89%82%E9%87%8F%E5%8F%8D%E5%BA%94 "分类:剂量反应") curve and is the preferred option from a scientific point of view. When a BMDL is used, the proper derivation of this BMDL should be established, considering crucial elements such as the choice of the critical effect size, the number of dose groups, the BMD credible interval (BMDL-BMDU) and the ratio between the BMDL and the lowest dose. Further considerations for deriving a BMDL from experimental animal studies is given in the section on in vivo qualification studies. If no reliable BMDL can be derived, a [NOAEL](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:NOAEL "分类:NOAEL") can be used as PoD. When no NOAEL has been established, a [LOAEL](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:LOAEL "分类:LOAEL") can be used as PoD.

BMDL可用作PoD。BMDL利用剂量-反应曲线上所有数据，从科学角度来看是首选选项。使用BMDL时，应验证该BMDL的正确推导，需考虑关键因素，如关键效应大小的选择、剂量组数量、BMD可信区间（BMDL-BMDU）以及BMDL与最低剂量的比值。从实验动物研究中推导BMDL的进一步考虑见体内资格研究章节。如果无法推导出可靠的BMDL，则可以使用NOAEL作为PoD。如果没有建立NOAEL，则可以使用LOAEL作为PoD。

The AL method is distinctive from the PDE method described in [ICH Q3C](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3C_R9_Guideline_on_impurities_guideline_for_residual_solvents_20240405 "EMA ICH Q3C R9 Guideline on impurities guideline for residual solvents 20240405") and [ICH Q3D](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3D_Elemental_impurities_Adopted_20190328 "EMA ICH Q3D Elemental impurities Adopted 20190328"), as it is meant to derive a product-specific limit for an impurity and not aimed at setting an authorised limit generally applicable for all products. This allows for a case-by-case approach that considers product-specific aspects and takes the body weight of the target population into account. Furthermore, the AL method includes corrections for bioavailability (as also done in Q3D) and considers uncertainty related to a surrogate approach, whereas the PDE method does not.

AL方法不同于ICH Q3C和ICH Q3D中描述的PDE方法，因为AL方法旨在推导特定产品中杂质的产品特异性限度，而不是设定适用于所有产品的通用授权限度。这允许采用个案处理的方法，考虑产品特异性因素，并考虑目标人群的体重。此外，AL方法包括对生物利用度的校正（如Q3D中所述），并考虑与替代方法相关的不确定性，而PDE方法则不包括这些。

## 5.6. New approach methodologies

5.6. 新方法学

New Approach Methodologies include in silico, _in chemico_ and in vitro approaches making use of existing data or applying non-animal models to characterise the hazard or risk of a compound. It is still an area under development with many stakeholders being active in developing new approach methodologies as an alternative to animal models, such as Animal-free Safety Assessment of Chemicals: Project Cluster for Implementation of Novel Strategies (ASPIS) (RISK-HUNT3R, ONTOX and PrecisionTox) and EPAA (European Partnership for Alternative Approaches).

新方法学包括利用现有数据或应用非动物模型进行危害或风险表征的计算机模拟（in silico）、化学模拟（in chemico）和体外（in vitro）方法。该领域仍在发展中，许多利益相关方正在积极开发替代动物模型的新方法学，例如无动物化学品安全评估项目集群（ASPIS）（RISK-HUNT3R、ONTOX和PrecisionTox）和欧洲替代方法伙伴关系（EPAA）。

### 5.6.1. Computational toxicology

5.6.1. 计算毒理学

If impurity-specific data or the read-across approach have not resulted in relevant data for qualifying the impurity, computational predictive in silico tools comprising of one or more endpoint-specific models can be used to identify potential safety alerts i.e., toxicophores of the impurity, or to further characterise an identified safety concern (see section 5.6.4.). Development of predictive models for [adverse reactions](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E4%B8%8D%E8%89%AF%E5%8F%8D%E5%BA%94 "分类:不良反应") has been described in literature and could provide a major advantage in reducing the need for animal testing to qualify an impurity (Bender et al., 2007).

如果杂质特异性数据或交叉读取方法未能获得用于杂质资格认定的相关数据，则可以使用包含一个或多个终点特异性模型的计算机预测in silico工具，以识别杂质的潜在安全警示（例如毒理基团），或进一步表征已识别的安全问题（见第5.6.4节）。文献中已描述了用于预测不良反应的预测模型的开发，这可能在减少杂质资格认定所需的动物试验方面具有重大优势（Bender等，2007）。

Computational toxicology refers to the use of computational, in silico methods to predict the potential toxicity of compounds without the need for traditional animal testing. This includes predictions of (Quantitative) Structure-Activity Relationships ((Q)SAR) that is usually based on trend analyses across broad databases and training sets of chemical compounds tested in vitro and in/ex vivo, which can be processed using Artificial Intelligence ([AI](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E4%BA%BA%E5%B7%A5%E6%99%BA%E8%83%BD "分类:人工智能")) and Machine Learning (ML) to analyze the data (Ajisafe et al_._ 2025).

计算毒理学是指使用计算机模拟（in silico）方法预测化合物潜在毒性，而无需传统动物试验。这包括（定量）构效关系（（Q）SAR）的预测，通常基于对广泛化合物数据库和体外及离体/体内测试训练集的化学化合物进行趋势分析，这些数据可以使用人工智能（AI）和机器学习（ML）进行处理和分析（Ajisafe等，2025）。

_**Validation of the model**_

_**模型的验证**_

When an in silico model is used in the risk assessment of an impurity, the validity of the model for the specific prediction should be justified by the Applicant. The model should follow the five validation principles for (Q)SAR models according to the Organisation for Economic Cooperation and Development (OECD), that is, the model should have 1) a defined endpoint; 2) an unambiguous algorithm; 3) a defined domain of applicability; 4) appropriate measures of goodness-of-fit, robustness and predictivity; and 5) a mechanistic interpretation, if possible (OECD, 2004). Other important guidance includes the OECD Guidance Document on the Validation of (Quantitative) Structure-Activity Relationship Model (OECD, 2014) and the OECD (Q)SAR Assessment Framework (OECD, 2023). The validity assessment should summarise the applicability of the model for the intended purpose, considering whether the pre-specified criteria for performance metrics (e.g. Matthew's correlation coefficient for binary predictors, MCC) and data interpretation are met, also defining potential limitations. In general, the key points for validating in silico prediction tools include, but are not limited to:

当计算机模型用于杂质风险评估时，申请人应证明该模型特定预测的有效性。该模型应遵循经济合作与发展组织（OECD）为（Q）SAR模型制定的五个验证原则，即模型应具备：1）明确的终点；2）明确的算法；3）明确的应用领域；4）适当的拟合优度、稳健性和预测能力的评估措施；5）可能情况下的机制解释（OECD，2004）。其他重要指南包括OECD发布的《（定量）构效关系模型验证指南文件》（OECD，2014）和OECD（Q）SAR评估框架（OECD，2023）。有效性评估应总结模型对于预期用途的适用性，考虑是否满足预先规定的性能指标（例如，二元预测器的Matthew相关系数，MCC）和数据解释标准，并定义潜在的局限性。通常，验证计算机预测工具的关键点包括但不限于：

- Cross-validation (appropriate cross-validation techniques, e.g. k-fold cross-validation)
    
    交叉验证（适当的交叉验证技术，例如k折交叉验证）
    

- Performance metrics (appropriate performance metrics relevant to the specific prediction task, such as accuracy, precision, ROC-AUC, MCC etc.)
    
    性能指标（与特定预测任务相关的适当性能指标，如准确率、精密度、ROC-AUC、MCC等）
    

- Benchmarking (comparison of the performance of the tool against existing methods or benchmarks to demonstrate its efficacy)
    
    基准测试（将工具的性能与现有方法或基准进行比较以证明其疗效）
    

- Interpretability (interpretable predictions for intended use)
    
    可解释性（针对预期用途的可解释预测）
    

- [Validation](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E9%AA%8C%E8%AF%81 "分类:验证") datasets (high-quality validation datasets that are representative of the problem domain and cover a wide range of scenarios)
    
    验证数据集（高质量、具有代表性且涵盖广泛场景的问题领域的验证数据集）
    

Several best practice papers for validation of (Q)SAR methods have also been published, e.g. in a recent paper by Ajisafe et al_.,_ which includes considerations of cross-validation, external validation, and benchmarking against traditional methods, as well as how to ensure model robustness and generalisability for ML/AI developed models (Ajisafe et al_._ 2025, Raies and Bajic 2016; Myatt et al_._ 2017, Worth et al., 2011).

也已发表了几篇关于（Q）SAR方法验证的最佳实践论文，例如Ajisafe等人最近发表的论文，其中包括交叉验证、外部验证和与传统方法的基准测试等方面的考虑，以及如何确保机器学习/人工智能开发模型的稳健性和泛化能力（Ajisafe等，2025；Raies和Bajic，2016；Myatt等，2017；Worth等，2011）。

It is the responsibility of the software provider to make the [validation report](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E9%AA%8C%E8%AF%81%E6%8A%A5%E5%91%8A "分类:验证报告") for the software tool available with specific descriptions of the above parameters, defining the applicability domain of the model and performance within the domain. The model validity, applicability and adequacy should be reported in the QSAR Model Reporting Format (QMRF). It is meanwhile the responsibility of the Applicant applying the tool to provide an expert review of the validity of the performed prediction, including that the target molecule is within the domain of applicability, which is typically reported in the QSAR Prediction Reporting Format (QPRF) (OECD, 2024). A summary of the validation assessment, e.g. in the form of a QPRF, should be submitted as an annex to the full in silico prediction report, including an expert review of the data, to conclude on the outcome and justify the validity of the prediction.

软件供应商有责任提供软件工具的验证报告，并对上述参数进行具体描述，定义模型的应用领域及其在该领域内的性能。应在QSAR模型报告格式（QMRF）中报告模型的有效性、适用性和充分性。同时，使用该工具的申请人有责任对预测的有效性进行专家评审，包括确认目标分子在应用领域范围内，这通常在QSAR预测报告格式（QPRF）中报告（OECD，2024）。验证评估的总结（例如，以QPRF形式）应作为附件提交至完整的计算机预测报告中，并包括对数据的专家评审，以得出结论并证明预测的有效性。

It is recommended to use the latest updated version of the tool available and to provide a full report of the in silico analysis in the submission. Of note, earlier versions may be justified (e.g. the tool has not undergone significant changes that affect prediction performance). Where available, the use of two complementary methods is recommended to enhance confidence in the prediction (statistical-based and expert rule-based). The absence of the complementary method for the chosen endpoint(s) should be justified.

建议使用最新版本的工具，并在提交材料中提供完整的计算机分析报告。需要注意的是，早期版本也可被接受（例如，该工具未经历影响预测性能的重大变更）。如有可能，建议使用两种互补的方法以增强预测的可信度（基于统计的方法和基于专家规则的方法）。若所选终点缺乏互补方法，应予以说明。

_**Quantitative Structure Activity Relationship (QSAR) tools to predict potential toxicophores**_

_**定量构效关系（QSAR）工具用于预测潜在毒理基团**_

In a first step, (Q)SAR tools can be used to identify potential safety concerns associated with the chemical structure or a portion of a structure (e.g., a functional group) of the impurity (toxicophores). The endpoints included should be able to define general (chronic) toxicity on major target organs and systems (liver, kidney, CVS, GIT, skin, CNS and RS), ideally in terms of functional and tissue organ changes. An alert for non-mutagenic carcinogenicity should also be considered and human relevance for mode of action should be addressed, e.g. via published literature and available tools/ databases to qualify the impurity. Any additional alert, e.g. for reproductive organs, reported by the chosen tool and not described among endpoints listed above, should be included in the expert review and its relevance considered in the overall level of concern. QSAR model predictions are most reliable if they come from the model's applicability domain. In case of an out-of-applicability-domain prediction for the impurity at the predefined endpoint(s), the prediction is not considered as reliable and an elaborated expert judgement with additional supporting evidence or alternative tools with a more suitable training dataset would be needed. For products administered topically on skin, a sensitising potential should be considered. Multiple predictive tools (commercial or free) are available for assessing e.g. general toxicity endpoints or skin sensitisation potential (reviewed by Teubner et al., 2013; Dik et al., 2014; Golden et al., 2020; Ta et al., 2021; Rim, 2020, Wei et al., 2024). It is also encouraged to use available open data sources and dashboards that enable access to collections of chemical hazard and risk information from public and governmental databases (Williams et al., 2017) as well as historical toxicological and [pharmacological](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%8D%AF%E7%90%86%E5%AD%A6 "分类:药理学") databases (Watford et al., 2019, Wang et al., 2017) to further determine whether the impurity has known toxicophores. Models for physicochemical and [PK](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%8D%AF%E4%BB%A3%E5%8A%A8%E5%8A%9B%E5%AD%A6 "分类:药代动力学") properties can be used to further define risks associated with the impurity along with identified toxicophores, e.g. in terms of anticipated absorption of the impurity and the potential impact on toxicity.

第一步，可以使用（Q）SAR工具识别与杂质化学结构或结构部分（例如，官能团）相关的潜在安全问题（毒理基团）。所包括的终点应能够定义对主要靶器官和系统的总体（慢性）毒性（肝脏、肾脏、心血管系统、胃肠道、皮肤、中枢神经系统和呼吸系统），理想情况下应以功能和组织器官变化的形式进行描述。也应考虑非致突变性致癌性的警示，并应通过已发表文献和可用工具/数据库对作用机制的人类相关性进行评估，以对杂质进行定性。由所选工具报告的任何其他警示，例如对生殖器官的影响，若未列于上述终点中，应在专家评审中予以包括，并在总体关注水平中考虑其相关性。QSAR模型预测在模型的应用领域内时最为可靠。若杂质在预定义终点上的预测超出应用领域范围，则该预测不被视为可靠，需要进行详细的专家判断，并提供额外的支持证据或使用具有更合适训练数据集的替代工具。对于局部应用于皮肤的产品，应考虑其致敏潜力。已有多种预测工具（商业或免费）可用于评估例如总体毒性终点或皮肤致敏潜力（由Teubner等人，2013；Dik等人，2014；Golden等人，2020；Ta等人，2021；Rim，2020；Wei等人，2024综述）。也鼓励使用现有的开放数据源和仪表板，这些资源可提供来自公共和政府数据库的化学品危害和风险信息（Williams等人，2017），以及历史毒理学和药理学数据库（Watford等人，2019；Wang等人，2017），以进一步确定杂质是否具有已知的毒理基团。物理化学和药代动力学（PK）性质的模型可用于进一步定义与已识别毒理基团相关的杂质风险，例如，预测杂质的吸收情况及其对毒性的潜在影响。

As stated above, the outcome of a prediction should be supported by an expert review of the data as well as an assessment of the validity of the outcome. While there is no adopted protocol for performing an in silico hazard assessment, several papers have been published by international consortia, including industry and regulatory experts, in an effort to develop in silico toxicological protocols to support hazard assessment of different target organ toxicities. Myatt et al. proposes a checklist for elements to consider as part of an expert review of a QSAR model result as well as how to report the outcome (Myatt et al., 2018) while Cayley et al. reviews common arguments used to resolve prediction scenarios made by complementary (Q)SAR models as part of the [ICH M7](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_M7_R2_Guideline_on_assessment_and_control_of_DNA_reactive_mutagenic_impurities_in_pharmaceuticals_to_limit_potential_carcinogenic_risk_20230718 "EMA ICH M7 R2 Guideline on assessment and control of DNA reactive mutagenic impurities in pharmaceuticals to limit potential carcinogenic risk 20230718") framework (Cayley et al. 2023). Additional literature addresses expert review of predictions for genetic toxicity (Hasselgren et al., 2019), liver toxicity (Bassan et al., 2021a), heart, kidney and lung toxicity (Bassan et al., 2021b) and carcinogenicity (Tice et al., 2021). These publications cover important points to consider when using in silico tools to predict organ toxicity and performing an expert review of the outcome. Overall, key aspects of an expert review include a) Resolving conflicts, when different in silico models give different predictions (e.g., positive vs. negative) to decide on a final conclusion; b) Validating positive and negative predictions to ensure they are not false positives/negative by investigating supporting evidence for the prediction's limitations; c) Interpreting ambiguous results to clarify inconclusive or equivocal predictions; d) Consider the scientific rationale to justify the final conclusion by considering the models' training sets, methodologies, and the specific structural features of the [impurity](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8 "分类:杂质") being evaluated; e) Ensuring applicability to the impurity in question by checking if similar structures or mechanisms were considered during model development.

如上所述，预测结果应有对数据的专家评审以及对结果有效性的评估作为支持。尽管目前尚无公认的计算机危害评估协议，但已有几篇由国际联盟（包括工业界和监管专家）发表的论文，旨在开发计算机毒理学协议以支持不同靶器官毒性的危害评估。Myatt等人提出了一份清单，列出了作为QSAR模型结果专家评审的一部分应考虑的要素以及如何报告结果（Myatt等人，2018），而Cayley等人则回顾了在ICH M7框架下使用互补（Q）SAR模型解决预测场景时常用的论证方法（Cayley等人，2023）。其他文献还涉及对遗传毒性（Hasselgren等人，2019）、肝脏毒性（Bassan等人，2021a）、心脏、肾脏和肺毒性（Bassan等人，2021b）以及致癌性（Tice等人，2021）预测的专家评审。这些出版物涵盖了使用计算机工具预测器官毒性和进行结果专家评审时应考虑的重要要点。总体而言，专家评审的关键方面包括：a）解决不同计算机模型给出不同预测（例如，阳性 vs. 阴性）时的冲突，以得出最终结论；b）验证阳性和阴性预测，通过调查预测局限性的支持证据确保其不是假阳性/阴性；c）解释模糊结果，以澄清不确定或模棱两可的预测；d）考虑模型的训练集、方法学以及所评估杂质的特定结构特征，以科学依据支持最终结论；e）通过检查模型开发过程中是否考虑了类似结构或机制，确保其适用于所讨论的杂质。

### 5.6.2. In vitro approaches

5.6.2. 体外方法

In vitro models can be helpful to fill data gaps, _e.g._ in vitro models for transport and metabolism can strengthen the predictivity of in silico tools for [bioavailability](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%94%9F%E7%89%A9%E5%88%A9%E7%94%A8%E5%BA%A6 "分类:生物利用度") (Paixão et al., 2012; Schneckener et al., 2019) or they can be used to compare the potency of impurities for a specific in vitro endpoint (Escher et al., 2022; Rovida et al., 2021).

体外模型可用于填补数据空白，例如，用于转运和代谢的体外模型可增强计算机工具对生物利用度的预测能力（Paixão等人，2012；Schneckener等人，2019），或可用于比较杂质在特定体外终点上的效力（Escher等人，2022；Rovida等人，2021）。

When (Q)SAR predictions raise concerns, further qualification data may be needed. Targeted use of in vitro methods (2D and 3D cell systems and micro-physiological systems) with careful selection of endpoints may be considered. Currently, no single assay would provide a definitive answer to the question whether an impurity can be considered safe at the specified level. Scientific efforts are ongoing to develop batteries and strategies for using in vitro approaches. When applying an in vitro approach to evaluate the safety of a NMI, assays should be carefully selected based on concerns identified from SAR or read-across analyses and their applicability justified. Targeted in vitro models might not be validated for their use for regulatory purposes. This should not prevent the use of non-standard in vitro methods. To facilitate an assessment of the quality of data produced and their potential utility in regulatory applications, supportive information should be provided, showing that the method is suitable for its intended purpose. Useful guidance to this end can be found in the Guideline on the principles of regulatory acceptance of 3Rs (replacement, reduction, refinement) testing approaches ([EMA/CHMP/CVMP/JEG-3Rs/450091/2012](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_Regulatory_acceptance_of_3R_\(replacement,_reduction,_refinement\)_testing_approaches_Adopted_guideline_20161222 "EMA Regulatory acceptance of 3R (replacement, reduction, refinement) testing approaches Adopted guideline 20161222")) currently under revision and the OECD Guidance document for describing non-guideline in vitro [test methods](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E5%88%86%E6%9E%90%E6%96%B9%E6%B3%95 "分类:分析方法") (OECD 2014).

当（Q）SAR预测引发担忧时，可能需要进一步的定性数据。可考虑使用体外方法（2D和3D细胞系统及微生理系统），并仔细选择终点指标。目前尚无单一的分析方法能够明确回答杂质在规定水平下是否可以被认为是安全的。科学界正在持续努力开发一系列方法和策略以使用体外方法。在应用体外方法评估NMI的安全性时，应根据SAR或交叉阅读分析中识别出的关注点仔细选择分析方法，并证明其适用性。靶向体外模型可能未经过验证可用于监管目的。这不应妨碍使用非标准体外方法。为便于评估所产生数据的质量及其在监管应用中的潜在效用，应提供支持性信息，以证明该方法适用于其预期用途。有关此方面的有用指南可参见《关于3Rs（替代、减少、优化）测试方法监管接受原则的指南》（EMA/CHMP/CVMP/JEG-3Rs/450091/2012，目前正修订中）以及OECD关于描述非指南体外测试方法的指南文件（OECD 2014）。

It is expected that more valuable information would be obtained from assays in which the purified NMI is tested. Adding spiked samples of the DS to test systems would complicate the interpretation of the read-outs, as the DS itself may also have an effect in the in vitro model employed.

预计通过测试纯化的NMI可以获得更有价值的信息。将原料药的加标样品加入测试系统会增加结果解读的复杂性，因为原料药本身在所使用的体外模型中也可能产生影响。

Several papers have been published in which industry consortia investigate and discuss secondary [pharmacology](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%8D%AF%E7%90%86%E5%AD%A6 "分类:药理学") in vitro screening panels for detecting off-target activity at relevant receptors and enzymes that are known to be associated with toxicities, such as different kinases, hERG, nuclear hormone receptors and G-protein coupled receptors. Specific targets for in vitro screenings are recommended in these papers to cover a broad range of organ toxicities in screening panels already used by industry in medicinal product development and lead optimisation (Bowes et al., 2012, Lynch et al., 2017, Blum et al., 2023, Brennan et al., 2024). The proposed targets in the papers, especially the more recent paper from Brennan et al., 2024, could form a suitable strategy for further in vitro testing to demonstrate lack of concern for associated toxicities for the NMI.

已有若干论文发表，其中工业联盟研究并讨论了用于检测脱靶活性的体外筛选面板，这些面板针对已知与毒性相关的受体和酶，如不同激酶、hERG、核激素受体和G蛋白偶联受体。这些论文中推荐了体外筛选的特定靶点，以涵盖工业在药品开发和先导化合物优化中已使用的筛选面板中的广泛器官毒性（Bowes等，2012年；Lynch等，2017年；Blum等，2023年；Brennan等，2024年）。这些论文中提出的靶点，特别是Brennan等（2024年）的最新论文，可为开展进一步体外测试以证明NMI无相关毒性担忧提供合适的策略。

### 5.6.3. Adverse Outcome Pathways (AOPs)

5.6.3. 不良结局路径（AOPs）

The AOP methodology is an approach that provides a framework to collect, organise and evaluate relevant information on biological and toxicological effects of chemicals. More specifically, the AOP approach organises existing knowledge concerning biologically plausible and empirically supported links between molecular-level perturbation of a biological system and an adverse outcome at a level of biological organisation of regulatory concern (OECD, 2017). An AOP can be used to combine evidence from several in silico and/or in vitro studies to fill in knowledge gaps regarding toxicological events. OECD has developed guidance on the development and assessment of AOPs as well as an AOP framework for organising data at the chemical and biological levels (OECD, 2017) where several AOPs addressing different targets have been included in the AOP Wiki.

AOP方法是一种提供框架以收集、组织和评估化学品生物学和毒理学效应相关信息的方法。更具体地说，AOP方法组织了关于生物系统分子水平扰动与监管关注的生物组织水平不良结果之间生物学上合理且经验支持的联系的现有知识（OECD，2017年）。AOP可用于结合多个体外和/或体外研究的证据，以填补毒理学事件的知识空白。OECD已制定了关于AOP开发和评估的指南以及一个用于组织化学品和生物水平数据的AOP框架（OECD，2017年），其中多个针对不同靶点的AOP已被纳入AOP Wiki。

If sufficiently justified, AOPs could be used to both qualitatively or quantitatively to qualify an NMI by either documenting the lack of hazard of a compound for major organ toxicities, or to derive a [NOAEL](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:NOAEL "分类:NOAEL") or BMDL for setting an AL.

如果能够充分论证，AOP可用于定性或定量地对NMI进行评估，以证明化合物对主要器官毒性的无危害性，或推导出NOAEL或BMDL以设定AL。

### 5.6.4. Hazard characterisation and quantitative risk estimation

5.6.4. 危害特征描述和定量风险评估

NAM primarily provide qualitative data, and their use in quantitative risk estimation remains limited. To enable quantitative interpretation — particularly, the translation of quantitative in vitro data to the in vivo situation (QIVIVE) — additional data and methodological development are needed. As a result, the current application of NAM tools is often focused on hazard identification and characterisation (Schmeisser et al., 2023). When NAM tools indicate the absence of relevant hazards for major targets (liver, kidney, CVS, GIT, CNS and respiratory system), this information can be incorporated in a weight-of-evidence approach for the safety assessment of the NMI. Conversely, if a potential hazard is identified using NAM tool, it must be demonstrated that the potency of the impurity to elicit the associated toxicity is not of concern. In such cases, it may be sufficient to justify that the exposure to the impurity at the proposed [specification](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%B4%A8%E9%87%8F%E6%A0%87%E5%87%86 "分类:质量标准") limit is without safety concerns based on a weight of evidence assessment of toxicity and PK considerations.

NAM主要提供定性数据，其在定量风险评估中的应用仍有限。为了实现定量解释 - 特别是将定量体外数据转化为体内情况（QIVIVE） - 需要额外的数据和方法学发展。因此，目前NAM工具的应用通常集中在危害识别和特征描述上（Schmeisser等，2023年）。当NAM工具表明主要靶点（肝脏、肾脏、心血管系统、胃肠道、中枢神经系统和呼吸系统）无相关危害时，这一信息可纳入NMI安全性评估的权重证据方法中。相反，如果使用NAM工具识别出潜在危害，则必须证明该杂质引发相关毒性的效力无须担忧。在这种情况下，可能足以证明基于毒性与药代动力学考虑的权重证据评估，杂质在拟定质量标准限值下的暴露量无安全性担忧。

## 5.7. In vivo qualification studies

5.7. 体内评估研究

### 5.7.1. Design of in vivo studies

5.7.1. 体内研究设计

Conducting additional in vivo studies to qualify the toxicological properties of new [impurities](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8 "分类:杂质"), as suggested in [ICH Q3A(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3A_\(R2\)_Impurities_in_new_drug_substances "EMA ICH Q3A (R2) Impurities in new drug substances"), is discouraged. This is in line with the 3Rs principles and the limited scientific relevance and reliability of such studies in this context. In particular, in vivo studies using impurity-spiked batches of the DS at dose levels at or below the NOAEL of the DS have historically provided limited additional toxicological insight. However, if a new NMI cannot be controlled below DST/[TTC](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:TTC "分类:TTC") thresholds, and all other alternative qualification approaches mentioned above have been exhausted, an in vivo study may be considered. In such cases, a preferred study design is proposed below to ensure harmonisation of the approach for deriving a PoD for setting an AL based on in vivo data.

根据ICH Q3A(R2)建议，不鼓励开展额外的体内研究以评估新杂质的毒理特性。这与3Rs原则一致，并且在此背景下，此类研究的科学相关性和可靠性有限。特别是，历史上使用在原料药中添加杂质批次进行的体内研究，在剂量水平等于或低于原料药NOAEL时，所提供的额外毒理学信息有限。然而，如果新NMI无法控制在DST/TTC阈值以下，并且上述所有其他替代评估方法均已尝试无果，则可考虑进行体内研究。在这种情况下，建议采用以下优选研究设计，以确保基于体内数据推导设定AL的PoD方法的一致性。

Several industry-led publications have investigated the preferred study design and have provided recommendations for an in vivo study design (Mitra et al., 2021; Slikkerveer et al., 2024). While some of the principles from these publications are endorsed, others require further reflection, particularly regarding ensuring sufficient exposure to the impurity to establish an adequate exposure margin relative to the proposed specification limit of the impurity. As noted in both papers, the commonly used test item is a DS batch spiked with impurity. However, this design is flawed as the impurity levels may not be sufficient to ensure an adequate ratio between the impurity exposure and the AL. This uncertainty may lead to repetition of in vivo studies, which is undesirable from both scientific and ethical perspectives. Therefore, it is recommended to conduct the in vivo study using neat samples of the impurity (i.e. isolated impurity with a purity of > 95%), allowing for direct evaluation of the effects of the impurity itself without confounding influences from the DS. Moreover, the in vivo study should be [GLP](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:GLP "分类:GLP") compliant, conducted following OECD test guideline 407, designed for 28 days of repeated dosing generally in rats via the clinical [route of administration](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%BB%99%E8%8D%AF%E9%80%94%E5%BE%84 "分类:给药途径"). For medicinal products intended for short term use, the duration of the in vivo study could be reduced to 14 days. No recovery period for the treated groups is needed, but a vehicle control group should be included. Finally, toxicokinetics ([TK](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%AF%92%E4%BB%A3%E5%8A%A8%E5%8A%9B%E5%AD%A6 "分类:毒代动力学")) should be included but maybe integrated in the main study e.g. as part of the high dose group, avoiding the need for a separate TK group.

若干由工业主导的出版物已研究并提出了优选的研究设计，并为体内研究设计提供了建议（Mitra等，2021年；Slikkerveer等，2024年）。尽管支持这些出版物中的一些原则，但其他原则仍需进一步思考，特别是关于确保杂质充分暴露，以相对于杂质拟定质量标准限值建立足够的暴露余量。正如两篇论文中指出的，常用的测试物品是添加了杂质的原料药批次。然而，这种设计存在缺陷，因为杂质水平可能不足以确保杂质暴露量与AL之间具有充分的比例。这种不确定性可能导致体内研究的重复，这从科学和伦理角度来看都是不可取的。因此，建议使用杂质的纯净样品（即纯度>95%的分离杂质）进行体内研究，以便在不受原料药干扰的情况下直接评估杂质本身的影响。此外，体内研究应符合GLP规范，按照OECD试验指南407进行，通常在大鼠中通过临床给药途径进行为期28天的重复给药。对于拟短期使用的药品，体内研究的持续时间可缩短至14天。治疗组无需恢复期，但应包括溶媒对照组。最后，应包括毒代动力学（TK）研究，但可以将其整合到主研究中，例如作为高剂量组的一部分，从而避免单独设立TK组。

Another issue is the dose selection and number of animals per group. The overall recommendation from the papers is to use five animals/sex/group, including a high and a low dose level, to allow for deriving a [NOAEL](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:NOAEL "分类:NOAEL") as PoD. The BMD approach has been deemed a scientifically more advanced method for deriving a PoD however, compared to the NOAEL approach by several authorities. For example, the European Food Safety Authority (EFSA) has published an updated guidance on the use of the benchmark dose approach in risk assessment (EFSA, 2022), which gives guidance on how to apply the principles. Considering this, and for designing in vivo studies to qualify impurities, it is recommended to include at least four treated dose groups (besides a vehicle group) as well as 3 analysable animals/sex/group to ensure sufficient study power for modelling the [dose-response](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E5%89%82%E9%87%8F%E5%8F%8D%E5%BA%94 "分类:剂量反应") data from the experimental animal studies. The principles of the EFSA BMD guidance on dose selection (EFSA, 2022) are recommended to ensure sufficient statistical relevance of the dose-response curve for deriving a BMDL. To qualify NMI, preliminary dose-range finding studies with the impurity are not recommended. Considering the usual AFs for deriving an AL, the BMDL used as PoD should be at least 500-fold higher than the anticipated AL using the clinical route of administration. When a different route of administration is used in the toxicology study, an appropriate multiple should be considered, taking into account the need to use A6 as an additional AF to account for differences in [bioavailability](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%94%9F%E7%89%A9%E5%88%A9%E7%94%A8%E5%BA%A6 "分类:生物利用度"). Generally, 3-fold increments are acceptable for spacing of the doses. The high dose may also be limited by the maximum tolerated dose.

另一个问题是剂量选择和每组动物数量。文献中的总体建议是每组每性别使用五只动物，包括高剂量和低剂量水平，以便确定NOAEL作为PoD。与NOAEL方法相比，BMD方法已被多个监管机构认为是科学上更为先进的PoD确定方法。例如，欧洲食品安全局（EFSA）已发布了关于在风险评估中使用基准剂量方法的更新指南（EFSA，2022），该指南提供了如何应用这些原则的指导。鉴于此，并为了设计用于杂质定性研究的体内研究，建议除溶媒组外至少包括四个给药剂量组，以及每组每性别3只可分析的动物，以确保实验动物研究中剂量应答数据建模具有足够的研究效能。建议采用EFSA关于剂量选择的BMD指南（EFSA，2022），以确保剂量应答曲线在统计学上的相关性，从而确定BMDL。为了定性NMI，不建议进行杂质的初步剂量范围探索研究。考虑到通常用于确定AL的安全系数（AF），作为PoD的BMDL应至少比通过临床给药途径预期的AL高500倍。当毒理学研究中使用不同的给药途径时，应考虑适当的倍数，并考虑到需要使用A6作为额外的安全系数（AF）以补偿生物利用度的差异。通常，剂量间隔采用3倍递增是可以接受的。高剂量也可能受到最大耐受剂量的限制。

_**Special considerations for oncology products**_

_**肿瘤产品特殊考虑**_

For products covered under [ICH S9](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_S9_Non-clinical_evaluation_for_anticancer_pharmaceuticals "EMA ICH S9 Non-clinical evaluation for anticancer pharmaceuticals"), the general approach to qualify NMIs as described above may not be applicable. In line with ICH S9, impurity qualification may instead be based on the similarity in safety profile between the impurity and the DS. This approach typically results in a low level of concern for most impurities in ICH S9 products (see Section 5.4.), and additional assessment is only warranted for impurities with a high level of concern; there is no need to control the impurity at a level where no toxicity is anticipated.

对于ICH S9涵盖的产品，上述用于界定NMI的一般方法可能不适用。根据ICH S9，杂质的界定可能基于杂质与原料药在安全性特征方面的相似性。这种方法通常导致ICH S9产品中大多数杂质的关注水平较低（见第5.4节），仅对高关注水平的杂质需要进一步评估；无需将杂质控制在预期无毒性的水平。

### 5.7.2. Setting limits based on in vivo data

5.7.2. 基于体内数据设定限度

Currently, no dedicated guidance has been issued by EMA or ICH regarding the derivation of a BMDL for use as PoD in establishing an AL for [impurities](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8 "分类:杂质"). Until such guidance becomes available, the EFSA guidance from 2022 may be consulted for key principles and methodological considerations. Based on the best fitted model and the most relevant BMDL, an AL can be derived following the approach outlined in Section 5.5. of this reflection paper.

目前，EMA或ICH尚未发布关于将BMDL作为杂质AL的PoD进行推导的专门指南。在指南发布之前，可参考EFSA 2022年的指南以获取关键原则和方法学考虑。根据最佳拟合模型和最相关的BMDL，可按照本思考性文件第5.5节所述方法推导AL。

## 5.8. Products under clinical development

5.8. 临床研究中的产品

Evaluation of NMIs in [investigational medicinal products](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%AF%95%E9%AA%8C%E7%94%A8%E8%8D%AF%E5%93%81 "分类:试验用药品") under clinical development should follow the principles outlined in [ICH M3(R2)](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_M3_\(R2\)_Non-clinical_safety_studies_for_the_conduct_of_human_clinical_trials_for_pharmaceuticals "EMA ICH M3 (R2) Non-clinical safety studies for the conduct of human clinical trials for pharmaceuticals"). The risk assessment may be supported by relevant sections of this reflection paper, as outlined above. Special consideration should be given to impurities of higher concern, such as those with known [structural alerts](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E8%AD%A6%E7%A4%BA%E7%BB%93%E6%9E%84 "分类:警示结构") or based on prior knowledge of related chemical classes. These may necessitate additional qualification efforts, such as lower batch-level usage or inclusion of supporting toxicological data. The level of concern analysis, as described in Section 5.4., can be a useful tool to determine the extent of qualification needed. Notably, short-term exposure in early-phase [clinical trials](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E4%B8%B4%E5%BA%8A%E7%A0%94%E7%A9%B6 "分类:临床研究") may serve as a de-risking factor, potentially reducing the need for extensive impurity qualification. If the levels of NMIs in clinical trial batches are assessed – using the level of concern analysis - to be of low concern no further qualification efforts would be expected.

临床研究中临床试验用药品的NMI评估应遵循ICH M3(R2)中所述原则。如上所述，本思考性文件的相关章节可作为风险评估的支持依据。应特别关注高关注杂质，例如具有已知结构警示的杂质或基于相关化学类别已有知识的杂质。这可能需要额外的界定工作，例如降低批次使用水平或纳入支持性毒理学数据。如第5.4节所述的关注水平分析可作为确定所需界定程度的有用工具。值得注意的是，早期临床试验中的短期暴露可能作为降低风险的因素，从而可能减少对广泛杂质界定的需求。如果通过关注水平分析评估临床试验批次中的NMI水平为低关注，则无需进一步界定工作。

# 6. Conclusion

6. 结论

When impurity-specific safety information for an NMI may be necessary, alternative strategies beyond the conduct of additional in vivo general toxicology studies to gather this information may be followed. These alternative strategies include the use of [TTC](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:TTC "分类:TTC"), (Q)SAR, read-across and in vitro approaches. This information can be used in an integrated risk assessment. A weight-of-evidence approach that includes all aspects that determine the level of concern, could be sufficient to decide that the NMI can be considered safe at the specified level.

当需要特定杂质的安全信息时，可采用除进行额外体内一般毒理学研究以外的替代策略来获取该信息。这些替代策略包括使用TTC、(Q)SAR、交叉读取和体外方法。这些信息可用于综合风险评估。包含决定关注水平的所有因素的证据权重方法，可能足以判断NMI在指定水平下是安全的。

_**Use of assessment factors**_

_**评估因子的使用**_

The use of modifying factors for the derivation of a [PDE](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:PDE "分类:PDE") is described in [ICH Q3C](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3C_R9_Guideline_on_impurities_guideline_for_residual_solvents_20240405 "EMA ICH Q3C R9 Guideline on impurities guideline for residual solvents 20240405")/[D](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3D_Elemental_impurities_Adopted_20190328 "EMA ICH Q3D Elemental impurities Adopted 20190328")/[E](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3E_Guideline_for_extractables_and_leachables_20250818 "EMA ICH Q3E Guideline for extractables and leachables 20250818") documents. Here, we reflect on the use of AFs, which are similar to the modifying factors described for the PDE methodology, but these reflections do not necessarily apply to the PDE methodology since for the derivation of an AL, product-specific considerations are taken into account.

关于如何使用调整因子推导PDE的内容在ICH Q3C/D/E文件中有描述。在此，我们对AF（评估因子）的使用进行了反思，AF与PDE方法中所述的修饰因子类似，但由于在制定AL时考虑了产品特定因素，因此这些反思不一定适用于PDE方法。

其中 M = 体重，常数 k 设为 10。常见实验动物的标准值可在ICH指南中找到。50 kg被用作人类的标准体重。在某些情况下，ICH文件中未提供数值，或者有理由进行偏离。在这些情况下，可以使用以下公式计算AF1：

**AF2 is a factor of 10 to account for variability between individuals**

**AF2是一个10倍因子，用于考虑个体之间的变异性**

A factor of 10 is generally applied ([ICH Q3C](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3C_R9_Guideline_on_impurities_guideline_for_residual_solvents_20240405 "EMA ICH Q3C R9 Guideline on impurities guideline for residual solvents 20240405")/[ICH Q3D](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3D_Elemental_impurities_Adopted_20190328 "EMA ICH Q3D Elemental impurities Adopted 20190328")). ICH Q3D provides the possibility to split this factor into a [toxicokinetic](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E6%AF%92%E4%BB%A3%E5%8A%A8%E5%8A%9B%E5%AD%A6 "分类:毒代动力学") and a toxicodynamic component (3.18 each) on a case-by-case basis. It is less likely that this option can be used to justify an AL for NMIs. It is difficult to justify that there is little or no toxicodynamic differences between individuals in the target population. Toxicokinetics of organic compounds is more complex than of [elemental impurities](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E5%85%83%E7%B4%A0%E6%9D%82%E8%B4%A8 "分类:元素杂质") and includes metabolism. Often it is not known if a potential toxicological hazard is attributable to the parent impurity or to a metabolite. Besides metabolism, variability in absorption, distribution, and elimination may exist within the target population.

通常应用10倍因子（ICH Q3C/ICH Q3D）ICH Q3D提供了一种按个案情况将该因子拆分为毒代动力学和毒效动力学部分（各3.18倍）的可能性。此选项用于证明NMIs的AL合理性可能性较低。很难证明目标人群个体之间在毒效动力学方面差异很小或没有差异。有机化合物的毒代动力学比元素杂质更复杂，包括代谢。通常不清楚潜在毒理学危害是源于母体杂质还是代谢物。除了代谢之外，目标人群中可能存在吸收、分布和消除方面的差异。

**AF3 is a variable factor to account for toxicity studies of short-term exposure**

**AF3是一个可变因子，用于考虑短期暴露毒性研究**

Specific values for F3 mentioned in Q3C/D, to be used when the PoD is taken from a non-chronic study, in principle are also applicable when deriving an AL for a specific product. However, some changes can be considered on a case-by-case basis, for instance taking into account that the product is administered intermittently, or only short-term (_i.e._ up to 1 month). In the case of medicinal products administered for less than a patient's lifetime, it may be appropriate to select a PoD from an animal study with a relatively short duration and use a lower value for AF3 than would usually be applied when a PDE is derived for chronic use. If additional animal studies are available with even longer duration, these may have BMDL/[NOAEL](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:NOAEL "分类:NOAEL") values based on findings that may not be relevant for shorter term exposures and therefore may not be the most appropriate PoD for a given DP.

Q3C/D中提到的F3特定值，当PoD取自非慢性研究时，原则上也可用于特定产品的AL推导。然而，可根据个案情况考虑进行某些调整，例如考虑到产品是间歇给药或仅短期给药（即最多1个月）对于给药时间短于患者寿命的药品，选择来自动物研究的相对较短持续时间的PoD，并使用比通常用于慢性使用PDE推导时更低的AF3值可能是合适的。如果还有更长持续时间的附加动物研究，这些研究可能基于与短期暴露无关的发现得出BMDL/NOAEL值，因此可能不是特定DP最合适的PoD。

**AF4 is a factor that may be applied in cases of severe toxicity, e.g., non-mutagenic carcinogenicity, neurotoxicity, or teratogenicity.**

**AF4是一个可在严重毒性情况下应用的因子，例如非致突变性致癌性、神经毒性或致畸性。**

This factor can be used to take into account the severity of an effect. Some examples for developmental toxicity are provided in ICH Q3C/D. When no BMDL or NOAEL is available and a [LOAEL](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:LOAEL "分类:LOAEL") is used at which the severe effect is observed, a high AF4 value up to 10 should be considered.

该因子可用于考虑效应的严重程度。ICH Q3C/D中提供了发育毒性的部分示例。当没有BMDL或NOAEL可用，并且使用观察到严重效应的LOAEL时，应考虑较高的AF4值，最高可达10。

The value for AF4 is always related to the severity of the [adverse effects](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E4%B8%8D%E8%89%AF%E5%8F%8D%E5%BA%94 "分类:不良反应") at the LOAEL that is chosen as critical endpoint. This is illustrated in the example below.

AF4的值始终与所选LOAEL处不良效应的严重程度相关。如下示例所示。

In this example, when choosing liver toxicity as a relevant endpoint, the PoD would be 1 mg/kg/day and AF4 would be 1. 数据缺失并不是将AF4设定为大于1的理由。例如，当缺乏发育毒性数据时，不应仅仅因为数据缺失无法排除致畸效应的可能性就将AF4设定为10。数据的缺失性和质量是任何风险评估中的不确定性分析部分都需要考虑的因素。

**AF5 is a variable factor that may be applied if the NOEL was not established**

**AF5 是一个可变因素，如果未建立 NOEL，则可以应用该因素**

[ICH Q3C](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3C_R9_Guideline_on_impurities_guideline_for_residual_solvents_20240405 "EMA ICH Q3C R9 Guideline on impurities guideline for residual solvents 20240405") indicates that a factor of up to 10 could be used depending on the severity of the toxicity if the no-effect level (NOEL) was not established. [ICH Q3D](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3D_Elemental_impurities_Adopted_20190328 "EMA ICH Q3D Elemental impurities Adopted 20190328") differentiates between NOAEL/LOAEL and NOEL/lowest observed effect level (LOEL). Both guidelines indicate that the severity/adversity of the effects plays a role in determining AF5. Yet, as discussed above, AF4 also addresses the severity of the effects observed. For AF5, the steepness of the dose-response curve is also relevant in choosing the values. In cases of a shallow [dose-response](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E5%89%82%E9%87%8F%E5%8F%8D%E5%BA%94 "分类:剂量反应") curve between the [NOAEL](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:NOAEL "分类:NOAEL") and the [LOAEL](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:LOAEL "分类:LOAEL") a higher AF5 of up to 5 may be appropriate. This will however also depend on the spacing between doses, where a close spacing between the NOAEL and LOAEL will lead to a high AF5, e.g. 5, and a wider dose spacing will lead to a lower AF5, e.g. 3-4. In cases of a steep dose-response curve between NOAEL and LOAEL, AF5 may be more moderate. Here, a close dose spacing could lead to a higher AF5, e.g. 2-3, while a wide dose spacing between NOAEL and LOAEL would lead to a low AF5, e.g. 1.

ICH Q3C 指出，如果未确定无作用水平 (NOEL)，可根据毒性的严重程度使用最高达 10 的因子。ICH Q3D 区分了 NOAEL/LOAEL 与 NOEL/最低观察效应水平 (LOEL)。两个指南均指出，效应的严重性/有害性在确定 AF5 时起一定作用。然而，如上所述，AF4 也涉及所观察效应的严重程度。对于 AF5，剂量-反应曲线的陡峭程度在选择数值时也具有相关性。在 NOAEL 和 LOAEL 之间剂量-反应曲线平缓的情况下，AF5 可适当高达 5。然而，这也取决于剂量之间的间距，NOAEL 和 LOAEL 之间剂量间距较小时可能导致较高的 AF5，例如 5，而剂量间距较大时则可能导致较低的 AF5，例如 3-4。在 NOAEL 和 LOAEL 之间剂量-反应曲线陡峭的情况下，AF5 可能较为适中。在这种情况下，NOAEL 和 LOAEL 之间剂量间距较小可能导致较高的 AF5，例如 2-3，而剂量间距较大则可能导致较低的 AF5，例如 1。

If an effect is observed at the PoD but is not considered adverse, this dose level is considered to be a NOAEL, but it also can be considered to be a LOEL. This should not lead to a value greater than 1 for AF5. The observed effect could be an adaptive response without any adverse sequelae. However, when the effect at the LOEL is related to the same process that leads to adversity at higher dose levels or with longer duration of exposure, it could be justifiable to assign an AF5 level greater than 1 (e.g. 3). If sufficient information is available, the choice of AF5 may also be informed by the distance between the LOAEL and the projected dose level where no [adverse effects](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E4%B8%8D%E8%89%AF%E5%8F%8D%E5%BA%94 "分类:不良反应") are seen. In case a BMDL is chosen as PoD and the BMDL is sufficiently justified, the value for AF5 would be 1.

如果在 PoD 观察到效应但不认为具有有害性，则该剂量水平被视为 NOAEL，但也可能被视为 LOEL。这不应导致 AF5 的数值大于 1。所观察到的效应可能是一种适应性反应，不会产生任何有害后果。然而，当 LOEL 处的效应与在较高剂量水平或更长暴露时间下导致有害效应的机制相关时，可以合理地为 AF5 赋予大于 1 的值（例如 3）。如果有足够的信息，AF5 的选择也可以根据 LOAEL 与预测的无有害效应剂量水平之间的差距来确定。如果选择了 BMDL 作为 PoD 并且 BMDL 得到了充分论证，则 AF5 的值为 1。

Based on these considerations, the choice of values for AF4 and AF5 are related to some extent. Choosing a value of 10 for both factors would only be appropriate when severe toxicity is observed at the LOAEL, and this dose level is used as PoD.

基于这些考虑，AF4 和 AF5 值的选择在某种程度上是相关的。只有当在 LOAEL 观察到严重毒性且该剂量水平被用作 PoD 时，才适合将两个因子均设定为 10。

**AF6 is a variable factor to account for route of exposure difference (e.g., oral versus parenteral).**

**AF6 是一个可变因子，用于考虑暴露途径差异（例如口服与肠胃外给药）。**

In the absence of data for the intended route of exposure and/or where data are available but not considered sufficient for a safety assessment for the [route of administration](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%BB%99%E8%8D%AF%E9%80%94%E5%BE%84 "分类:给药途径"), a modifying factor can be used to correct for a difference in [bioavailability](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%94%9F%E7%89%A9%E5%88%A9%E7%94%A8%E5%BA%A6 "分类:生物利用度") between the route used in the study from which the PoD is taken and the bioavailability for the route for which an AL is being derived. Ideally, AF6 should be based on bioavailability of the parent compound. If a radiolabel study is used, it should be referred to as absorption because it is not clear if the radiolabel is the parent, or a metabolite, or a combination of parent and metabolite(s). For example, when adequate data suggests that the oral bioavailability is 30% and the PoD is taken from an oral toxicity study whereas the product is administered parenterally, AF6 would be 3. Alternatively, default factors can be applied when the bioavailability estimate is uncertain (due to conflicting data, the use of data with limited reliability, or dependence on bioavailability data for a surrogate compound). When using oral toxicity data to derive a parenteral AL conversion factor similar to [Q3D](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=EMA_ICH_Q3D_Elemental_impurities_Adopted_20190328 "EMA ICH Q3D Elemental impurities Adopted 20190328") can be used:

在缺乏预期暴露途径的数据和/或现有数据不足以进行该给药途径的安全性评估时，可以使用修正因子来校正从 PoD 所在研究中使用的途径与目标 AL 所在途径之间的生物利用度差异。理想情况下，AF6 应基于母体化合物的生物利用度。如果使用放射性标记研究，应将其称为吸收率，因为尚不清楚放射性标记的是母体化合物、代谢物，还是母体化合物与代谢物的组合。例如，当充分数据表明口服生物利用度为 30%，而 PoD 来自于口服毒性研究，而产品是通过肠胃外途径给药时，AF6 为 3。当生物利用度估计存在不确定性时（由于数据冲突、使用可靠性有限的数据，或依赖替代化合物的生物利用度数据），可以应用默认因子。当使用口服毒性数据推导肠胃外 AL 时，可以使用类似于 Q3D 的转换因子：

AF6= 100 Oral bioavailability <1%: divide by a modifying factor of 100;

AF6= 100 口服生物利用度 <1%：用 100 的修正因子进行除法运算；

AF6= 10 Oral bioavailability ≥ 1% and <50%: divide by a modifying factor of 10;

AF6= 10 口服生物利用度 ≥ 1% 且 <50%：用 10 的修正因子进行除法运算；

AF6= 2 Oral bioavailability ≥50% and <90%: divide by a modifying factor of 2; and

AF6= 2 口服生物利用度 ≥50% 且 <90%：用 2 的修正因子进行除法运算；以及

AF6=1 Oral bioavailability ≥ 90%: divide by a modifying factor of 1.

AF6=1 口服生物利用度 ≥ 90%：用 1 的修正因子进行除法运算。

In the absence of in vivo data, a NAM approach – combining in vitro data estimating oral absorption and internal clearance, with an in silico PBPK model - can be used to generate data for assessing bioavailability. The reliability of such models should be documented. When the compound is out of the applicability domain of the model, or when the reliability index is too low, the result of the model should be discarded. When sufficiently justified, the results from a NAM approach in regulatory submissions can be considered by the authorities.

在缺乏体内数据的情况下，可以采用 NAM 方法——结合体外数据估算口服吸收和体内清除率，并结合体外 PBPK 模型——来生成用于评估生物利用度的数据。应记录此类模型的可靠性。当化合物超出模型的适用域，或可靠性指数过低时，应舍弃模型的结果。在充分论证的情况下，监管机构可以考虑将 NAM 方法的结果用于监管申报。

Where appropriate bioavailability data were not available, and in lieu of NAM-derived estimates of bioavailability, a default modifying factor of 100 is suggested for AF6. Smaller values need further justification, e.g. reasoning based on the physicochemical characteristics of the compound. In addition, evidence of a clear biological response after oral exposure in toxicity studies can be leveraged to support a smaller AF6. When suitable bioavailability data are available for a surrogate molecule, allowing a read-across approach, these data may be leveraged to inform the bioavailability estimate, if sufficiently justified.

在缺乏适当的生物利用度数据时，并且作为对NAM推导的生物利用度估计值的替代，建议AF6采用默认修正因子100。较小的数值需要进一步论证，例如基于化合物理化特性的推理。此外，在毒性研究中经口暴露后出现明确生物学反应的证据可用于支持采用较小的AF6。当有替代分子的合适生物利用度数据可用时，允许采用交叉读取方法，这些数据可用于推导生物利用度估计值，但需充分论证。

When the data concerns an inhalation toxicology study, data on respiratory tract deposition, respiratory absorption rate and pulmonary metabolism may inform on AF6. If such data are not available and a parenteral AL needs to be derived, the value for AF6 needs justification, e.g. based on physico-chemical properties. If a compound shows local toxicity in the absence of systemic toxicity, the dose at which these effects are observed is less suitable to derive a parenteral AL. In contrast, when the medicinal product is administered by inhalation and no inhalation toxicology data are available for the leachable, as a cautious approach, 100% bioavailability of the external dose can be assumed, and the inhalation AL would be the same as the parenteral AL. When data can be presented that show [bioavailability](https://lib.shilinx.com/wiki/index.php?title=%E5%88%86%E7%B1%BB:%E7%94%9F%E7%89%A9%E5%88%A9%E7%94%A8%E5%BA%A6 "分类:生物利用度") is less, this could justify a smaller AF6.

当数据涉及吸入毒理学研究时，呼吸道沉积数据、呼吸道吸收率和肺部代谢数据可为AF6提供依据。如果此类数据不可用且需要推导肠胃外AL，则AF6的数值需要进行论证，例如基于理化性质。如果某化合物在没有系统毒性的情况下表现出局部毒性，则观察到这些效应的剂量不太适合用于推导肠胃外AL。相反，当药品通过吸入给药且可沥滤物没有吸入毒理学数据时，作为一种谨慎的方法，可以假设外部剂量的生物利用度为100%，此时吸入AL将与肠胃外AL相同。当有数据显示生物利用度较低时，这可以为使用较小的AF6提供依据。

Likewise, when systemic toxicity data observed in a dermal toxicity study are used to derive a parenteral AL and data on absorption are available after dermal exposure, AF6 can be based on these absorption data. In the absence of actual absorption data, AF6 needs to be justified, e.g. based on physiochemical characteristics of the compound and the formulation.

同样，当在皮肤毒性研究中观察到系统毒性数据并用于推导肠胃外AL，且有经皮肤暴露后的吸收数据时，AF6可以基于这些吸收数据。在缺乏实际吸收数据的情况下，AF6需要进行论证，例如基于化合物和制剂的理化特性。

**AF7 is a variable factor that may be applied if a read-across strategy is used.**

**AF7是一个可变因子，当采用交叉读取策略时可能会应用。**

When read-across strategy is utilised, a factor of up to 5 could be used depending on the level of (dis)similarity. In general, when a surrogate is considered similar based on the criteria described in this guideline, an AF7 of 1 may be applicable.

当采用交叉读取策略时，可根据相似性程度使用最高达5的因子。通常，当根据本指南中描述的标准认为替代物相似时，AF7可采用1。
